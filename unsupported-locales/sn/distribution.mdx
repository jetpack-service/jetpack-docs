# Maitiro eJetPack Tokens

Sisitimu yeJetPack token inosanganisira **kuburitswa uye kugoverwa kwetoken kunoenderana nemabasa**, **sisitimu yehuwandu hwemasevhisi**, **mibairo yevashandisi uye nzira dzekushandisa token**, uye **mekanizimu yekutenga nekugovera zvakare token**. Chimiro ichi chinopa **hurongwa hwakaringana uye hunoshanda hwemibairo** kune vashandisi nevapi vemasevhisi.

## Mechanism yeKugoverwa kweToken (Drop)

### Huwandu Hwetoken Hwakazara uye Nguva Yekugovera

- **Huwandu Hwakazara**: JetPack ichaburitsa huwandu hwakazara hwe **1 bhiriyoni Jet** tokens.
- **Nguva Yekugovera**: Tokens ichagoverwa kwe **38 makore**, ne **2,776 kugoverwa** kuchiitika panguva dzakafanana ne **Cardano Epoch** (1 epoch = 5 mazuva).

### Huwandu HweKugoverwa kweToken paEpoch

#### Kuverenga Huwandu HweKugoverwa Kubva paLogarithmic Function

Huwandu hwetokens hunogoverwa paepoch hwakagadzirirwa kudzikira nekufamba kwenguva. **Logarithmic function** inoshandiswa kuverenga huwandu hwekugoverwa kweepoch imwe neimwe. **1 Jet yakaenzana ne1,000,000 Pack**, saka huwandu hwekugoverwa hunoverengwa kusvika **panzvimbo yechitanhatu yedesimari** uye kubhadharwa zvinoenderana.

1. **Tsananguro yeLogarithmic Function $ f(t) $**:

   $$
   f(t) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

   Apo:

    - $ t $ inoverengwa seepoch nhamba, $ t = 1, 2, 3, \dotsc, 2776 $.
    - $ \ln $ ibasa re logarithm rechisikigo.

2. **Kuverenga Huwandu HweSum yeLog Function $ f(t) $ Pamusoro peEpoch Dzese**:

   Huwandu hwese hwebasa pamusoro penguva yese yekugovera $ S $ ndeye:

   $$
   S = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} f(t) = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

3. **Kuverenga Scaling Constant $ A $**:

   Huwandu hwese hwetoken hwe **1 bhiriyoni Jet** hunopatsanurwa ne $ S $ kuti kugadzirise huwandu hwekugovera kweepoch imwe neimwe:

   $$
   A = \dfrac{1,000,000,000}{S} = 2,412,005.7263929145
   $$

4. **Kuverenga Huwandu HweKugoverwa kweToken paEpoch**:

   Huwandu hwekugoverwa kweepoch imwe neimwe ndeye:

   $$
   \text{Distribution Amount}_t = A \times f(t) = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

5. **Mienzaniso yeHuwandu HweKugoverwa**:

   Kuverenga huwandu hwekugoverwa kweepoch dzekutanga:

    - **Epoch 1**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_1 = 3,479,788.70 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(1 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 2**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_2 = 2,195,502.227 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 3**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_3 = 1,739,894.35 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(4)}
      $$

   Huwandu hwekugoverwa kweepoch imwe neimwe hunotsanangurwa neukoshi hwebasa relog rinowirirana nenhamba yeepoch.

**Zvimiro zveKugoverwa**

- Sezvo $ \ln(t + 1) $ ichiwedzera sezvo $ t $ ichiwedzera, denominator inova huru, zvichikonzera kuti huwandu hwekugoverwa huderere zvishoma nezvishoma.
- Huwandu hwekugoverwa kweepoch imwe neimwe hunoderera nekufamba kwenguva, huchiswedera kuzero.
- Dhizaini iyi inokurudzira vatori vechikamu vekutanga uye inowedzera kushomeka kwetoken.

### Zvinodiwa Zvekugoverwa kweToken paSevhisi

#### Kuverenga Ratios Pakati peMasevhisi

Chiyero chekugoverwa kweimwe neimwe sevhisi chinoverengwa zvichibva pachiyero chebasa rayo $ P_n $ zvichienzaniswa nehuwandu hwechiyero chebasa $ P $:

$$
\text{Ratio for Service } n = \frac{P_n}{P}
$$

Apo:

- $ P_n $: Chiyero chebasa chesevhisi $ n $.
- $ P $: Huwandu hwezviyero zvemabasa emasevhisi ese.

#### Kuverenga Huwandu HweKugoverwa kweSevhisi

Huwandu hwetokens hunogamuchirwa nesevhisi $ n $ muepoch imwe neimwe $ S_n $ ndeye:

$$
S_n = \min\left(f_{\text{total}}(t) \times \left( \frac{P_n}{P} \right), \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n\right)
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t) $: Huwandu hwakagadziriswa hwekugoverwa kweepoch.
- $ \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n $: Huwandu hwetoken hwakanyanya hwakagoverwa zvichienderana nelevel yesevhisi (ona kugoverwa kwelevel-based maximum token pazasi).

#### Kubata kweTokens Dzisina Kugoverwa

##### Kutakura Tokens Dzisina Kugoverwa

- Tokens dzisina kugoverwa kubva kuepoch imwe neimwe **dzinoverengerwa** kuhuwandu hwekugoverwa kweepoch inotevera.
- Izvi zvinovimbisa kugoverwa kwetoken kunoshanda kunyangwe kana kutora chikamu kwekutanga kwevashandisi kuchiri kushoma.

##### Kuverenga Huwandu Hwese Hwakagadziriswa Hwekugoverwa kweEpoch

Huwandu hwese hwekugoverwa kweepoch inotevera ndeye:

$$
f_{\text{total}}(t+1) = A \times f(t+1) + \text{Undistributed Tokens from Previous Epoch}
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t+1) $: Huwandu hwakagadziriswa hwekugoverwa kweepoch $ t+1 $.
- $ A \times f(t+1) $: Huwandu hwekugoverwa zvichienderana nebasa rekutanga rekugovera.
- **Tokens Dzisina Kugoverwa Dzakatakurwa**: Huwandu hwetokens dzisina kugoverwa muepoch $ t $.

#### Kuverenga Chiyero cheBasa ($ P_n $) paSevhisi

Chiyero chebasa chesevhisi imwe neimwe chinoverengwa zvichibva pamametric anotevera. Kurema kwemetric imwe neimwe kunogona kugadziriswa kuburikidza nehutungamiri.

1. **JetPack Token Consumption ($ M_{1n} $)**
    - **Kurema**: $ w_1 = 50\% $
    - **Tsananguro**: Huwandu hwese hwetokens dzeJetPack dzinoshandiswa nevashandisi mukati mesevhisi.

2. **Degen Bunny Vote Count ($ M_{2n} $)**
    - **Kurema**: $ w_2 = 30\% $
    - **Tsananguro**: Huwandu hwevhoti dzakagamuchirwa kubva kuOG Degen Bunnies.

3. **Active User Count ($ M_{3n} $)**
    - **Kurema**: $ w_3 = 20\% $
    - **Tsananguro**: Huwandu hwevashandisi vakasiyana vakawana uye vakashanda mukati mesevhisi panguva yeepoch.

Chiyero chebasa chesevhisi imwe neimwe $ P_n $ chinoverengwa se:

$$
P_n = w_1 \times \left( \frac{M_{1n}}{M_{1\text{total}}} \right) + w_2 \times \left( \frac{M_{2n}}{M_{2\text{total}}} \right) + w_3 \times \left( \frac{M_{3n}}{M_{3\text{total}}} \right)
$$

Apo:

- $ M_{1n} $: Kushandiswa kwetoken dzeJetPack kwesevhisi $ n $.
- $ M_{1\text{total}} $: Kushandiswa kwetoken dzeJetPack kwese kumasevhisi ese.
- $ M_{2n} $: Huwandu hwevhoti dzeDegen Bunny kwesevhisi $ n $.
- $ M_{2\text{total}} $: Huwandu hwevhoti dzeDegen Bunny kumasevhisi ese.
- $ M_{3n} $: Huwandu hwevashandisi vakasiyana kwesevhisi $ n $.
- $ M_{3\text{total}} $: Huwandu hwevashandisi vakasiyana kumasevhisi ese.

**Muenzaniso**:

- **Sevhisi A**:
    - $ M_{1A} = 500 $
    - $ M_{2A} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3A} = 200 $
- **Sevhisi B**:
    - $ M_{1B} = 300 $
    - $ M_{2B} = 200 $
    - $ M_{3B} = 100 $
- **Sevhisi C**:
    - $ M_{1C} = 200 $
    - $ M_{2C} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3C} = 700 $

**Huwandu**:

- $ M_{1\text{total}} = 500 + 300 + 200 = 1000 $
- $ M_{2\text{total}} = 150 + 200 + 150 = 500 $
- $ M_{3\text{total}} = 200 + 100 + 700 = 1000 $

**Kurema**:

- $ w_1 = 0.5 $
- $ w_2 = 0.3 $
- $ w_3 = 0.2 $

**Kuverenga Chiyero cheBasa cheSevhisi A**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_A &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{500}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.5 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.2 \\
&= 0.25 + 0.09 + 0.04 \\
&= 0.38
\end{align*}
$$

**Kuverenga Chiyero cheBasa cheSevhisi B**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_B &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{300}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{200}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{100}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.3 + 0.3 \times 0.4 + 0.2 \times 0.1 \\
&= 0.15 + 0.12 + 0.02 \\
&= 0.29
\end{align*}
$$

**Kuverenga Chiyero cheBasa cheSevhisi C**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_C &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{700}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.2 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.7 \\
&= 0.10 + 0.09 + 0.14 \\
&= 0.33
\end{align*}
$$

Saka, zviyero zvemabasa ndezvi:

- **Sevhisi A**: $ P_A = 0.38 $
- **Sevhisi B**: $ P_B = 0.29 $
- **Sevhisi C**: $ P_C = 0.33 $

#### Zvinodiwa zveLevel uye Huwandu HweKugoverwa kweToken

Zvichienderana nechiyero chebasa $ P_n $, sevhisi imwe neimwe inopatsanurwa kuita **Levels 1 kusvika 9**. Iyi kupatsanurwa inoshandisa **cumulative top percentile** yezviyero zvemabasa emasevhisi ese.

| Level 3   | Top 23%                       | 20,000                               |
| Level 4   | Top 40%                       | 15,000                               |

##### Nzira Yekutsanangura Level

1. **Verenga Zviyero zveBasa**

   Shandisa nzira yakatsanangurwa kare kuverenga chiyero chebasa chesevhisi imwe neimwe $ P_n $.

2. **Tsanangura Zvibodzwa zveSevhisi**

   Rangarira masevhisi ese mu **kudzikira kwekurongeka** zvichienderana nezviyero zvemabasa avo.

3. **Verenga Cumulative Top Percentiles**

   Kune sevhisi imwe neimwe, verenga cumulative top percentile:

   $$
   \text{Cumulative Top Percentile (\%)} = \left(1 - \frac{\text{Rank} - 1}{N} \right) \times 100\%
   $$

   Apo:

    - $ \text{Rank} $: Chinzvimbo chesevhisi (kutanga kubva pa1).
    - $ N $: Huwandu hwese hwemasevhisi.

4. **Govera Levels**

   Govera levels zvichienderana necumulative top percentiles:

    - **Level 1**: Cumulative top percentile > 96%
    - **Level 2**: 89% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 96%
    - **Level 3**: 77% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 89%
    - **Level 4**: 60% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 77%
    - **Level 5**: 40% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 60%
    - **Level 6**: 23% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 40%
    - **Level 7**: 11% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 23%
    - **Level 8**: 4% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 11%
    - **Level 9**: Cumulative top percentile ≤ 4%

**Muenzaniso**:

Nehuwandu hwe **3** masevhisi:

1. **Verenga Zvibodzwa uye Cumulative Top Percentiles**

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{1 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 100\%
          $$

2. **Govera Levels**

## Mibairo yeVashandisi uye Kushandiswa kweToken

### **Mechanism yeMibairo yeVashandisi**

- **Mibairo Inoenderana neMabasa**: Vashandisi vanogamuchira tokens dzeJetPack kana vachiita **mabasa akabvumiranwa kare** (semuenzaniso, kugadzira zvemukati, kutamba mitambo) mukati mesevhisi.
- **Zvinodiwa zveMibairo yeToken**: Huwandu hwetokens hunopihwa pamabasa ega ega **hunotsanangurwa kuburikidza nekubvumirana** pakati pesevhisi nemushandisi weJetPack ecosystem.

### **Nzira dzekushandisa Token**

1. **Mukati meSevhisi**:
    - **Kuwana Zvimiro Zvekubhadhara**: Shandisa tokens kuwana zvimiro kana zvemukati zvinobhadharwa mukati mesevhisi.
    - **Kutenga Zvinhu**: Tenga zvinhu zvedhijitari kana zvinhu mukati mesevhisi uchishandisa tokens.

2. **Kushandisa JetPack Wallet Service Exchange**:
    - **Kutengesa Tokens**: Vashandisi vanogona kuchinjanisa tokens dzavo ne **ADA** (mari ye **Cardano**).
    - **Kugadzirisa Wallet**: JetPack wallet inobvumira kugadzirisa kwakachengeteka kwetokens uye kutumira kune mamwe masevhisi.

### **Mabhenefiti Anotarisirwa kubva Mukukosheswa kweToken**

- **Liquidity yeToken**: Kutengesa tokens pamisika kunowedzera **liquidity** yavo.
- **Mabhenefiti Eehupfumi**: Vashandisi vanogona kuwana **mabhenefiti ehupfumi ekuwedzera** nekutengesa tokens kana kukosha kwavo kuchiwedzera.

## Kutenga uye Kugovera Zvakare kweToken

### **Kutenga Tokens Zvakawedzerwa neMasevhisi**

- **Kana Tokens Dzagoverwa Dzapera**: Kana sevhisi yashandisa tokens dzese dzakagoverwa, inogona kutenga tokens dzakawedzerwa kubva ku **JetPack Wallet Service Exchange**.
- **Mari Yekutenga**: Tokens dzinotengwa uchishandisa **ADA**.
- **Kushandiswa kweTokens Dzakatengwa**: Tokens dzakatengwa dzinogona kushandiswa **kupa mibairo yevashandisi** kana **mukati mesevhisi**.

### **Tokens Dzinotengeswa uye Dzisingatengeswi**

- **Tokens Dzagoverwa** (**Dzisingatengeswi**): Tokens dzakagoverwa kumasevhisi **hadzigone kutengeswa** uye dzinongoshandiswa **kupa mibairo yevashandisi**.
- **Tokens Dzakatengwa uye Tokens Dzakatambirwa kubva kuVashandisi** (**Dzinotengeswa**): Tokens dzakatengwa neADA uye tokens dzakagamuchirwa kubva kuvashandisi **dzinogona kutengeswa**, zvichibatsira mukuwana **liquidity**.

### **Kugovera Zvakare kweToken uye Kusimudzira Ecosystem**

1. **Chimiro Chekutenderera kweToken**:
    - Masevhisi anopa vashandisi mibairo yetokens; vashandisi vanoshandisa mukati mesevhisi kana kutengesa pamisika.
    - Masevhisi anowana tokens kubva kuvashandisi kana kutenga kuti achengetedze **hurongwa hwemibairo hunogara**.

2. **Mhedzisiro yeKusimudzira Ecosystem**:
    - Kutenderera kwetoken kunosimudzira **kutorwa kwevashandisi** uye **kusimudzira sevhisi**.
    - Izvi zvinobatsira **kugadzikana kwekukosha kwetoken** uye **kukura kunogara kweecosystem**.
