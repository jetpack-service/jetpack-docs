# Imephu Yomgwaqo Yesevisi ye-JetPack

Umbono we-JetPack Service uwukufeza “**Ukuhlalisana phakathi Kwabasebenzisi Nezinsizakalo**.”

**Isigaba 1** sichaza izici ezibalulekile nezikhathi zokuphothulwa kwezinhlelo zokusebenza kuzo zonke izigaba, kusukela ku-Stage-1 kuya ku-Stage-5. Kulesi sigaba, zonke izici ezidingekayo zokuhlanganiswa okungenazihibe phakathi kohlelo lwe-JetPack nezinsizakalo zalo zizothuthukiswa, kugxilwe ekuqaliseni izinsizakalo eziyinhloko ezishayelwa yi-AI ngokulandelana. Isigaba ngasinye siqondiswa umgomo we-**“AI-First Service Initiative”**.

**Isigaba 2** sizoqala uma i-JetPack Service isibonise inani elanele. Lesi sigaba sizokwethula uhlelo lwezikhangiso olusekelwe kumathokheni lwe-JetPack nokuhlanganiswa nezinkundla zangaphandle ezifana ne-metaverse ne-e-commerce. Uhlelo oluningiliziwe luzomenyezelwa kamuva.


## Isigaba 1

### **Isigaba-1: Isigaba 'Gagarin'**
**Usuku Lokuqala**: Januwari 1, 2025

- Ukuthuthukiswa kohlelo lokuphatha izinsizakalo
  - Izici zokubhalisa nokususa izinsizakalo
  - Ukusetshenziswa kohlelo lokusatshalaliswa kwamathokheni
- Ukuthuthukiswa kwewebhusayithi kanye ne-REST API eyisisekelo

**Isigaba Siqanjwe Ngemva**: Yuri Gagarin, umuntu wokuqala esibhakabhakeni

---

### **Isigaba-2: Isigaba 'Shepard'**
**Isikhathi Esilindelekile**: Q3 2025

- Ukwethulwa kohlelo lokusebenza lwe-diary **haru**
- Umsebenzi wokukhishwa kwethokheni **OG Degen Bunny**
- Isici sokudlulisa amathokheni e-JetPack phakathi kwabasebenzisi
- Ukuhlanganiswa kokusekelwa kwesikhwama sangaphandle

**Isigaba Siqanjwe Ngemva**: Alan Shepard, umMelika wokuqala esibhakabhakeni

### **Isigaba-3: Isigaba 'Tereshkova'**
**Isikhathi Esilindelekile**: Q1 2026

- Ukwethulwa kwe-**Prompt Service**
- Ukuthuthukiswa kwe-**JetPack Token Exchange (CEX)**
- Ukuthuthukiswa kwe-**NFT Drop Service**
- Ukwandiswa kwe-SDK yezilimi eziningi

**Isigaba Siqanjwe Ngemva**: Valentina Tereshkova, owesifazane wokuqala esibhakabhakeni

### **Isigaba-4: Isigaba 'Armstrong'**
**Isikhathi Esilindelekile**: Q3 2026

- Ukwethulwa kwezinsizakalo ezihlobene ne-NFT

  - Ukusetshenziswa kwezinhlelo zokwenza izinqumo ezizimele

**Isigaba Siqanjwe Ngemva**: Neil Armstrong, umuntu wokuqala ukubeka unyawo enyangeni

### **Isigaba-5: Isigaba 'Leonov'**
**Isikhathi Esilindelekile**: Q1 2027

  - Izethameli eziqondiwe: abafundi baseyunivesithi, onjiniyela abazimele, njll.

  - Ukuqinisa ukuzinza nokuphepha kohlelo
