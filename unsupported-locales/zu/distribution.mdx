# Indlela AmaJetPack Tokens Asebenza Ngayo

Uhlelo lweJetPack token luqukethe **ukukhishwa kwe-token okusekelwe kumsebenzi nokusatshalaliswa**, uhlelo lwezinga lenkonzo, **imivuzo yomsebenzisi nezindlela zokusebenzisa i-token**, kanye **nomshini wokuthenga nokusatshalaliswa kabusha kwama-token**. Lesi sakhiwo sihlinzeka **ngohlelo lokuvuza olufanele nolusebenzayo** kubasebenzisi kanye nabahlinzeki bezinsizakalo.

## Indlela Yokusatshalaliswa Kwe-Token (Drop)

### Inani Eliphelele Le-Token Nesikhathi Sokusatshalaliswa

- **Inani Eliphelele**: IJetPack izokhipha inani eliphelele lama-**1 billion Jet** tokens.
- **Isikhathi Sokusatshalaliswa**: Ama-tokens azosatshalaliswa ngaphezu kweminyaka **38**, ngokuhlangana okuphelele kwe-**2,776** okwenzeka ngezikhathi ezifanayo ne-**Cardano Epoch** (1 epoch = 5 izinsuku).

### Inani Lokusatshalaliswa Kwe-Token nge-Epoch

#### Ukubala Inani Lokusatshalaliswa Ngokusekelwe Kumsebenzi We-Logarithmic

Inani lama-tokens elisatshalaliswa nge-epoch lihlelwe ukuthi lehle ngokuhamba kwesikhathi. Umsebenzi we-**logarithmic** usetshenziselwa ukubala inani lokusatshalaliswa nge-epoch ngayinye. **1 Jet ilingana no-1,000,000 Pack**, ngakho-ke amanani okusatshalaliswa abalwa kuze kufike endaweni yesithupha yeshumi futhi akhokhelwe ngokufanele.

1. **Incazelo Yomsebenzi We-Logarithmic $ f(t) $**:

   $$
   f(t) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

   Lapho:

    - $ t $ inombolo ye-epoch, $ t = 1, 2, 3, \dotsc, 2776 $.
    - $ \ln $ umsebenzi we-logarithm yemvelo.

2. **Ukubala Isamba Somsebenzi We-Log $ f(t) $ Phezu Kwazo Zonke I-Epochs**:

   Isamba esiphelele samagugu omsebenzi ngaso sonke isikhathi sokusatshalaliswa $ S $ siyi:

   $$
   S = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} f(t) = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

3. **Ukubala Okuguquguqukayo Kokulinganisa $ A $**:

   Inani eliphelele le-token le-**1 billion Jet** lihlukaniswa ngesamba $ S $ ukuze kulungiswe inani lokusatshalaliswa nge-epoch ngayinye:

   $$
   A = \dfrac{1,000,000,000}{S} = 2,412,005.7263929145
   $$

4. **Ukubala Inani Lokusatshalaliswa Kwe-Token nge-Epoch**:

   Inani lokusatshalaliswa nge-epoch ngayinye liyi:

   $$
   \text{Distribution Amount}_t = A \times f(t) = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

5. **Izibonelo Zamanani Okusatshalaliswa**:

   Ukubala amanani okusatshalaliswa kwe-epoch yokuqala:

    - **Epoch 1**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_1 = 3,479,788.70 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(1 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 2**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_2 = 2,195,502.227 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 3**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_3 = 1,739,894.35 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(4)}
      $$

   Inani lokusatshalaliswa kwe-epoch ngayinye linqunywa ngenani lomsebenzi we-log elihambisana nenombolo ye-epoch.

**Izici Zokusatshalaliswa**

- Njengoba $ \ln(t + 1) $ likhula njengoba $ t $ likhula, isihlukanisi siba sikhulu, okubangela ukuthi inani eliphelele lokusatshalaliswa lehle kancane kancane.
- Inani lokusatshalaliswa nge-epoch liyancipha ngokuhamba kwesikhathi, lisondele ku-zero.
- Lo mklamo ukhuthaza ababambiqhaza bokuqala futhi ukhuphula ukushoda kwe-token.

### Izinkomba Zokusatshalaliswa Kwe-Token Nge-Service

#### Ukubala Izilinganiso Phakathi Kwezinsizakalo

Isilinganiso sokusatshalaliswa kwenkonzo ngayinye sibalwa ngokusekelwe kumphumela wayo wokusebenza $ P_n $ uma kuqhathaniswa nesamba somphumela wokusebenza $ P $:

$$
\text{Ratio for Service } n = \frac{P_n}{P}
$$

Lapho:

- $ P_n $: Umphumela wokusebenza kwenkonzo $ n $.
- $ P $: Isamba semiphumela yokusebenza yazo zonke izinsizakalo.

#### Ukubala Inani Lokusatshalaliswa Kwenkonzo Ngayinye

Inani lama-tokens inkonzo $ n $ elithola nge-epoch ngayinye $ S_n $ liyi:

$$
S_n = \min\left(f_{\text{total}}(t) \times \left( \frac{P_n}{P} \right), \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n\right)
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t) $: Inani eliphelele lokusatshalaliswa elilungisiwe le-epoch.
- $ \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n $: Inani eliphezulu le-token elinikezwe ngokusekelwe ezingeni lenkonzo (bona ukusatshalaliswa kwe-token okukhulu okusekelwe ezingeni ngezansi).

#### Ukuphathwa Kwama-Tokens Angasatshalaliswanga

##### Ukuthwala Phezu Kwama-Tokens Angasatshalaliswanga

- Ama-tokens angasatshalaliswanga nge-epoch ngayinye **engezwa** enanini eliphelele lokusatshalaliswa le-epoch elandelayo.
- Lokhu kuqinisekisa ukusatshalaliswa kwe-token okusebenzayo ngisho nalapho ukubamba iqhaza kwenkonzo yokuqala kuphansi.

##### Ukubala Inani Eliphelele Lokusatshalaliswa Elilungisiwe Nge-Epoch

Inani eliphelele lokusatshalaliswa le-epoch elandelayo liyi:

$$
f_{\text{total}}(t+1) = A \times f(t+1) + \text{Undistributed Tokens from Previous Epoch}
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t+1) $: Inani eliphelele lokusatshalaliswa elilungisiwe le-epoch $ t+1 $.
- $ A \times f(t+1) $: Inani lokusatshalaliswa elisekelwe kumsebenzi oyisisekelo wokusatshalaliswa.
- **Ama-Tokens Angasatshalaliswanga Athwalwe Phezu**: Inani lama-tokens angasatshalaliswanga nge-epoch $ t $.

#### Ukubala Umphumela Wokusebenza ($ P_n $) Nge-Service

Umphumela wokusebenza kwenkonzo ngayinye ubalwa ngokusekelwe kulawa mapharamitha alandelayo. Isisindo sepharamitha ngayinye singalungiswa ngokusebenzisa ukuphatha.

1. **Ukusetshenziswa Kwe-JetPack Token ($ M_{1n} $)**
    - **Isisindo**: $ w_1 = 50\% $
    - **Incazelo**: Inani eliphelele lama-JetPack tokens asetshenziswa abasebenzisi ngaphakathi kwenkonzo.

2. **Inani Lezivoti Ze-Degen Bunny ($ M_{2n} $)**
    - **Isisindo**: $ w_2 = 30\% $
    - **Incazelo**: Inani lezivoti ezitholwe ku-OG Degen Bunnies.

3. **Inani Labasebenzisi Abasebenzayo ($ M_{3n} $)**
    - **Isisindo**: $ w_3 = 20\% $
    - **Incazelo**: Inani labasebenzisi abahlukile abathole ukufinyelela futhi abasebenzayo enkosini ngesikhathi se-epoch.

Umphumela wokusebenza kwenkonzo ngayinye $ P_n $ ubalwa kanje:

$$
P_n = w_1 \times \left( \frac{M_{1n}}{M_{1\text{total}}} \right) + w_2 \times \left( \frac{M_{2n}}{M_{2\text{total}}} \right) + w_3 \times \left( \frac{M_{3n}}{M_{3\text{total}}} \right)
$$

Lapho:

- $ M_{1n} $: Ukusetshenziswa kwe-JetPack token kwenkonzo $ n $.
- $ M_{1\text{total}} $: Ukusetshenziswa kwe-JetPack token okuphelele kuzo zonke izinsizakalo.
- $ M_{2n} $: Inani lezivoti ze-Degen Bunny kwenkonzo $ n $.
- $ M_{2\text{total}} $: Inani lezivoti ze-Degen Bunny eliphelele kuzo zonke izinsizakalo.
- $ M_{3n} $: Inani labasebenzisi abasebenzayo kwenkonzo $ n $.
- $ M_{3\text{total}} $: Inani labasebenzisi abasebenzayo eliphelele kuzo zonke izinsizakalo.

**Isibonelo**:

- **Inkonzo A**:
    - $ M_{1A} = 500 $
    - $ M_{2A} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3A} = 200 $
- **Inkonzo B**:
    - $ M_{1B} = 300 $
    - $ M_{2B} = 200 $
    - $ M_{3B} = 100 $
- **Inkonzo C**:
    - $ M_{1C} = 200 $
    - $ M_{2C} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3C} = 700 $

**Izamba**:

- $ M_{1\text{total}} = 500 + 300 + 200 = 1000 $
- $ M_{2\text{total}} = 150 + 200 + 150 = 500 $
- $ M_{3\text{total}} = 200 + 100 + 700 = 1000 $

**Izisindo**:

- $ w_1 = 0.5 $
- $ w_2 = 0.3 $
- $ w_3 = 0.2 $

**Ukubala Umphumela Wokusebenza Kwenkonzo A**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_A &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{500}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.5 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.2 \\
&= 0.25 + 0.09 + 0.04 \\
&= 0.38
\end{align*}
$$

**Ukubala Umphumela Wokusebenza Kwenkonzo B**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_B &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{300}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{200}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{100}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.3 + 0.3 \times 0.4 + 0.2 \times 0.1 \\
&= 0.15 + 0.12 + 0.02 \\
&= 0.29
\end{align*}
$$

**Ukubala Umphumela Wokusebenza Kwenkonzo C**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_C &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{700}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.2 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.7 \\
&= 0.10 + 0.09 + 0.14 \\
&= 0.33
\end{align*}
$$

Ngakho-ke, imiphumela yokusebenza imi kanje:

- **Inkonzo A**: $ P_A = 0.38 $
- **Inkonzo B**: $ P_B = 0.29 $
- **Inkonzo C**: $ P_C = 0.33 $

##### Indlela Yokunquma Izinga

1. **Bala Imiphumela Yokusebenza**

   Sebenzisa indlela echazwe ngaphambili ukubala umphumela wokusebenza kwenkonzo ngayinye $ P_n $.

   Beka zonke izinsizakalo ngokulandelana kwehla ngokusekelwe emiphumeleni yazo yokusebenza.

3. **Bala Amaphesenti Aphezulu Aqoqa**

   Kwinkonzo ngayinye, bala iphesenti eliphezulu eliqoqiwe:

   $$
   \text{Cumulative Top Percentile (\%)} = \left(1 - \frac{\text{Rank} - 1}{N} \right) \times 100\%
   $$

   Lapho:

    - $ \text{Rank} $: Isikhundla senkonzo (siqala ku-1).
    - $ N $: Inani eliphelele lezinsizakalo.

4. **Nika Izinga**

    - **Izinga 4**: 60% < Iphesenti eliphezulu eliqoqiwe ≤ 77%
    - **Izinga 5**: 40% < Iphesenti eliphezulu eliqoqiwe ≤ 60%
    - **Izinga 6**: 23% < Iphesenti eliphezulu eliqoqiwe ≤ 40%
    - **Izinga 7**: 11% < Iphesenti eliphezulu eliqoqiwe ≤ 23%
    - **Izinga 8**: 4% < Iphesenti eliphezulu eliqoqiwe ≤ 11%
    - **Izinga 9**: Iphesenti eliphezulu eliqoqiwe ≤ 4%

## Imivuzo Yomsebenzisi Nezindlela Zokusebenzisa I-Token

### **Indlela Yemivuzo Yomsebenzisi**

- **Imivuzo Esekelwe Kumsebenzi**: Abasebenzisi bathola ama-JetPack tokens uma benza **imisebenzi evunyelwene ngaphambilini** (isb., ukudala okuqukethwe, ukudlala) ngaphakathi kwenkonzo.
- **Izinkomba Zemivuzo Ye-Token**: Inani lama-tokens anikezwa imisebenzi ngayinye **linqunywa ngokubonisana** phakathi kwenkonzo nomsebenzisi we-JetPack ecosystem.

### **Izindlela Zokusebenzisa I-Token**

1. **Ngaphakathi Kwenkonzo**:
    - **Ukufinyelela Izici Ezikhokhelwayo**: Sebenzisa ama-tokens ukufinyelela izici ezikhokhelwayo noma okuqukethwe ngaphakathi kwenkonzo.
    - **Thenga Izimpahla**: Thenga izimpahla zedijithali noma izinto ezinkonzweni usebenzisa ama-tokens.

2. **Ukusebenzisa I-JetPack Wallet Service Exchange**:
    - **Ukuthengisa Ama-Tokens**: Abasebenzisi bangashintshanisa ama-tokens abo nge-**ADA** (imali ye-**Cardano**).
    - **Ukuphathwa Kwe-Wallet**: I-JetPack wallet ivumela ukuphathwa okuphephile kwama-tokens nokudluliselwa kwezinye izinsizakalo.

### **Izinzuzo Ezilindelekile Ekukhuleni Kwenani Le-Token**

- **Uketshezi Lwe-Token**: Ukushintshaniswa kwama-tokens ezinkampanini zokushintshanisa kwandisa **uketshezi** lwawo.
- **Inzuzo Yomnotho**: Abasebenzisi bangathola **izinzuzo zomnotho ezengeziwe** ngokuthengisa ama-tokens uma inani lawo likhula.

## Ukuthenga Nokusatshalaliswa Kabusha Kwe-Token

### **Ukuthengwa Kwe-Token Okungeziwe Ngezinsizakalo**

- **Uma Ama-Tokens Asatshalaliswa Ephelelwa**: Uma inkonzo isebenzisa wonke ama-tokens anikezwe, ingathenga ama-tokens angeziwe ku-**JetPack Wallet Service Exchange**.
- **Imali Yokuthenga**: Ama-tokens athengwa usebenzisa **ADA**.
- **Ukusetshenziswa Kwama-Tokens Athengiwe**: Ama-tokens athengiwe angasetshenziswa **ukuvuza abasebenzisi** noma **ngaphakathi kwenkonzo**.

### **Ama-Tokens Angathengiseki vs. Ama-Tokens Angathengiseka**

- **Ama-Tokens Asatshalaliswa** (**Angathengiseki**): Ama-tokens anikezwe izinsizakalo **awakwazi ukuthengiswa kabusha** futhi awenzelwe kuphela **imivuzo yomsebenzisi**.
- **Ama-Tokens Athengiwe Nama-Tokens Atholwe Kubasebenzisi** (**Angathengiseka**): Ama-tokens athengwa nge-ADA nama-tokens atholwe kubasebenzisi **angathengiswa kabusha**, okusiza ekutholeni **uketshezi**.

### **Ukusatshalaliswa Kabusha Kwe-Token Nokusebenza Kwe-Ecosystem**

1. **Isakhiwo Sokusatshalaliswa Kwe-Token**:
    - Izinsizakalo zivuza abasebenzisi ngama-tokens; abasebenzisi bawasebenzisa ngaphakathi kwezinsizakalo noma bawathengise ezinkampanini zokushintshanisa.
    - Izinsizakalo zithola ama-tokens kubasebenzisi noma zithenge ukuze zigcine **uhlelo lokuvuza oluzinzile**.

2. **Imiphumela Yokusebenza Kwe-Ecosystem**:
    - Ukusatshalaliswa kwe-token kukhuthaza **ukubamba iqhaza komsebenzisi** nokusebenza kwezinsizakalo.
    - Lokhu kunikela **ekuzinzeni kwenani le-token** nokukhula okuqhubekayo kwe-ecosystem.
