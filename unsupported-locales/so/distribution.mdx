# Sida Ay U Shaqeeyaan Token-yada JetPack

Nidaamka token-ka JetPack wuxuu ka kooban yahay **soo saarista iyo qaybinta token-ka ku saleysan hawlaha**, nidaam heer adeeg, abaalmarinno isticmaalaha iyo hababka isticmaalka token-ka, iyo habka iibinta iyo dib-u-qaybinta token-ka. Qaab-dhismeedkan wuxuu bixiyaa nidaam abaalmarin oo caddaalad ah oo wax ku ool ah labadaba isticmaalayaasha iyo bixiyeyaasha adeegga.

## Habka Qaybinta Token-ka (Drop)

### Wadarta Bixinta Token-ka iyo Mudada Qaybinta

- **Wadarta Bixinta**: JetPack waxay soo saari doontaa wadarta guud ee **1 bilyan Jet** token.
- **Mudada Qaybinta**: Token-yada waxaa la qaybin doonaa muddo **38 sano ah**, iyadoo wadarta guud ee **2,776 qaybinta** ay ka dhici doonaan isla muddada **Cardano Epoch** (1 epoch = 5 maalmood).

### Qaddarka Qaybinta Token-ka ee Epoch kasta

#### Xisaabinta Qaddarka Qaybinta iyadoo la adeegsanayo Shaqada Logarithmic

Qaddarka token-yada ee la qaybiyo epoch kasta waxaa loogu talagalay inuu hoos u dhaco waqti ka dib. Shaqo **logarithmic** ah ayaa loo isticmaalaa in lagu xisaabiyo qaddarka qaybinta ee epoch kasta. **1 Jet wuxuu u dhigmaa 1,000,000 Pack**, sidaas darteed qaddarka qaybinta waxaa loo xisaabiyaa ilaa **lixaad meel meel** waxaana la bixiyaa si waafaqsan.

1. **Qeexida Shaqada Logarithmic $ f(t) $**:

   $$
   f(t) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

   Halka:

    - $ t $ uu yahay lambarka epoch, $ t = 1, 2, 3, \dotsc, 2776 $.
    - $ \ln $ waa shaqada logarithm dabiiciga ah.

2. **Xisaabinta Wadarta Shaqada Log $ f(t) $ Dhammaan Epoch-yada**:

   Wadarta guud ee qiimaha shaqada ee muddada qaybinta oo dhan $ S $ waa:

   $$
   S = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} f(t) = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

3. **Xisaabinta Joogtada Miisaaminta $ A $**:

   Wadarta guud ee token-ka ee **1 bilyan Jet** waxaa loo qaybiyaa wadarta $ S $ si loo hagaajiyo qaddarka qaybinta ee epoch kasta:

   $$
   A = \dfrac{1,000,000,000}{S} = 2,412,005.7263929145
   $$

4. **Xisaabinta Qaddarka Qaybinta Token-ka ee Epoch kasta**:

   Qaddarka qaybinta ee epoch kasta waa:

   $$
   \text{Distribution Amount}_t = A \times f(t) = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

5. **Tusaalooyinka Qaddarka Qaybinta**:

   Xisaabinta qaddarka qaybinta ee epoch-yada hore:

    - **Epoch 1**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_1 = 3,479,788.70 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(1 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 2**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_2 = 2,195,502.227 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 3**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_3 = 1,739,894.35 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(4)}
      $$

   Qaddarka qaybinta ee epoch kasta waxaa go'aamiya qiimaha shaqada log ee u dhigma lambarka epoch.

**Astaamaha Qaybinta**

- Maadaama $ \ln(t + 1) $ uu kordho marka $ t $ uu kordho, qaybiyaha wuxuu noqdaa mid weyn, taasoo keenta in qaddarka qaybinta guud uu si tartiib tartiib ah u yaraado.
- Qaddarka qaybinta ee epoch kasta wuxuu hoos u dhacaa waqti ka dib, isagoo ku dhowaanaya eber.
- Naqshadeyntan waxay dhiirigelisaa ka qaybgalayaasha hore waxayna kordhisaa yaraanta token-ka.

### Shuruudaha Qaybinta Token-ka ee Adeeg kasta

#### Xisaabinta Saamiyada Dhexdeeda Adeegyada

Saamiga qaybinta ee adeeg kasta waxaa lagu xisaabiyaa iyadoo lagu saleynayo dhibcaha waxqabadka $ P_n $ marka loo eego wadarta dhibcaha waxqabadka $ P $:

$$
\text{Ratio for Service } n = \frac{P_n}{P}
$$

Halka:

- $ P_n $: Dhibcaha waxqabadka ee adeegga $ n $.
- $ P $: Wadarta dhibcaha waxqabadka ee dhammaan adeegyada.

#### Xisaabinta Qaddarka Qaybinta ee Adeeg kasta

Qaddarka token-yada ee adeegga $ n $ uu helo epoch kasta $ S_n $ waa:

$$
S_n = \min\left(f_{\text{total}}(t) \times \left( \frac{P_n}{P} \right), \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n\right)
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t) $: Qaddarka qaybinta guud ee la hagaajiyay ee epoch-ka.
- $ \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n $: Qaddarka ugu badan ee token-ka ee loo qoondeeyay iyadoo lagu saleynayo heerka adeegga (eeg qaybinta ugu badan ee token-ka ee ku saleysan heerka hoose).

#### Maareynta Token-yada aan la Qaybin

##### Qaadista Token-yada aan la Qaybin

- Token-yada aan la qaybin ee epoch kasta waxaa lagu **darsadaa** qaddarka qaybinta guud ee epoch-ka xiga.
- Tani waxay hubisaa qaybinta token-ka oo wax ku ool ah xitaa marka ka qaybgalka adeegga hore uu hooseeyo.

##### Xisaabinta Qaddarka Qaybinta Guud ee la Hagaajiyay ee Epoch kasta

Qaddarka qaybinta guud ee epoch-ka xiga waa:

$$
f_{\text{total}}(t+1) = f(t+1) + \text{Undistributed Tokens from Previous Epoch}
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t+1) $: Qaddarka qaybinta guud ee la hagaajiyay ee epoch $ t+1 $.
- $ f(t+1) $: Qaddarka qaybinta ee ku saleysan shaqada qaybinta aasaasiga ah.
- **Token-yada aan la Qaybin ee la Qaaday**: Tirada token-yada aan la qaybin ee epoch $ t $.

#### Xisaabinta Dhibcaha Waxqabadka ($ P_n $) ee Adeeg kasta

Dhibcaha waxqabadka ee adeeg kasta waxaa lagu xisaabiyaa iyadoo lagu saleynayo cabbirrada soo socda. Miisaanka cabbir kasta waxaa lagu hagaajin karaa iyada oo loo marayo maamul.

1. **Isticmaalka Token-ka JetPack ($ M_{1n} $)**
    - **Miisaanka**: $ w_1 = 50\% $
    - **Sharaxaad**: Wadarta guud ee token-yada JetPack ee ay isticmaaleen isticmaalayaasha adeegga gudihiisa.

2. **Tirada Codadka Degen Bunny ($ M_{2n} $)**
    - **Miisaanka**: $ w_2 = 30\% $
    - **Sharaxaad**: Tirada codadka laga helay OG Degen Bunnies.

3. **Tirada Isticmaalayaasha Firfircoon ($ M_{3n} $)**
    - **Miisaanka**: $ w_3 = 20\% $
    - **Sharaxaad**: Tirada isticmaalayaasha gaarka ah ee helay oo firfircoon adeegga inta lagu jiro epoch-ka.

Dhibcaha waxqabadka ee adeeg kasta $ P_n $ waxaa lagu xisaabiyaa sidan:

$$
P_n = w_1 \times \left( \frac{M_{1n}}{M_{1\text{total}}} \right) + w_2 \times \left( \frac{M_{2n}}{M_{2\text{total}}} \right) + w_3 \times \left( \frac{M_{3n}}{M_{3\text{total}}} \right)
$$

Halka:

- $ M_{1n} $: Isticmaalka token-ka JetPack ee adeegga $ n $.
- $ M_{1\text{total}} $: Wadarta isticmaalka token-ka JetPack ee dhammaan adeegyada.
- $ M_{2n} $: Tirada codadka Degen Bunny ee adeegga $ n $.
- $ M_{2\text{total}} $: Wadarta codadka Degen Bunny ee dhammaan adeegyada.
- $ M_{3n} $: Tirada isticmaalayaasha firfircoon ee adeegga $ n $.
- $ M_{3\text{total}} $: Wadarta isticmaalayaasha firfircoon ee dhammaan adeegyada.

**Tusaale**:

- **Adeegga A**:
    - $ M_{1A} = 500 $
    - $ M_{2A} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3A} = 200 $
- **Adeegga B**:
    - $ M_{1B} = 300 $
    - $ M_{2B} = 200 $
    - $ M_{3B} = 100 $
- **Adeegga C**:
    - $ M_{1C} = 200 $
    - $ M_{2C} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3C} = 700 $

**Wadarta**:

- $ M_{1\text{total}} = 500 + 300 + 200 = 1000 $
- $ M_{2\text{total}} = 150 + 200 + 150 = 500 $
- $ M_{3\text{total}} = 200 + 100 + 700 = 1000 $

**Miisaanka**:

- $ w_1 = 0.5 $
- $ w_2 = 0.3 $
- $ w_3 = 0.2 $

**Xisaabinta Dhibcaha Waxqabadka ee Adeegga A**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_A &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{500}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.5 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.2 \\
&= 0.25 + 0.09 + 0.04 \\
&= 0.38
\end{align*}
$$

**Xisaabinta Dhibcaha Waxqabadka ee Adeegga B**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_B &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{300}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{200}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{100}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.3 + 0.3 \times 0.4 + 0.2 \times 0.1 \\
&= 0.15 + 0.12 + 0.02 \\
&= 0.29
\end{align*}
$$

**Xisaabinta Dhibcaha Waxqabadka ee Adeegga C**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_C &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{700}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.2 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.7 \\
&= 0.10 + 0.09 + 0.14 \\
&= 0.33
\end{align*}
$$

Sidaas darteed, dhibcaha waxqabadka waa:

- **Adeegga A**: $ P_A = 0.38 $
- **Adeegga B**: $ P_B = 0.29 $
- **Adeegga C**: $ P_C = 0.33 $

#### Shuruudaha Go'aaminta Heerka iyo Qaddarka Qaybinta Ugu Badan ee Token-ka

Iyadoo lagu saleynayo dhibcaha waxqabadka $ P_n $, adeeg kasta waxaa loo kala saaraa **Heerarka 1 ilaa 9**. Kala soocidan waxay isticmaashaa **boqolkiiba sare ee urursan** ee dhammaan dhibcaha waxqabadka adeegyada.

##### Habka Go'aaminta Heerka

1. **Xisaabi Dhibcaha Waxqabadka**

   Isticmaal habka hore loo qeexay si aad u xisaabiso dhibcaha waxqabadka ee adeeg kasta $ P_n $.

2. **Go'aami Kala-saarista Adeegyada**

   Kala saar dhammaan adeegyada **si hoos u dhac ah** iyadoo lagu saleynayo dhibcaha waxqabadka.

3. **Xisaabi Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan**

   Adeeg kasta, xisaabi boqolkiiba sare ee urursan:

   $$
   \text{Cumulative Top Percentile (\%)} = \left(1 - \frac{\text{Rank} - 1}{N} \right) \times 100\%
   $$

   Halka:

    - $ \text{Rank} $: Kala-saarista adeegga (laga bilaabo 1).
    - $ N $: Wadarta tirada adeegyada.

4. **U Qoondee Heerarka**

   U qoondee heerarka iyadoo lagu saleynayo boqolkiiba sare ee urursan:

    - **Heerka 1**: Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan > 96%
    - **Heerka 2**: 89% < Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 96%
    - **Heerka 3**: 77% < Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 89%
    - **Heerka 4**: 60% < Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 77%
    - **Heerka 5**: 40% < Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 60%
    - **Heerka 6**: 23% < Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 40%
    - **Heerka 7**: 11% < Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 23%
    - **Heerka 8**: 4% < Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 11%
    - **Heerka 9**: Boqolkiiba sare ee urursan ≤ 4%

**Tusaale**:

Iyadoo la adeegsanayo wadarta guud ee **3** adeeg:

1. **Xisaabi Kala-saarista iyo Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan**

    - **Adeegga A**:

        - Dhibcaha Waxqabadka: $ P_A = 0.38 $
        - Kala-saarista: 1
        - Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{1 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 100\%
          $$

    - **Adeegga C**:

        - Dhibcaha Waxqabadka: $ P_C = 0.33 $
        - Kala-saarista: 2
        - Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{2 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 66.67\%
          $$

    - **Adeegga B**:

        - Dhibcaha Waxqabadka: $ P_B = 0.29 $
        - Kala-saarista: 3
        - Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{3 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 33.33\%
          $$

2. **U Qoondee Heerarka**

    - **Adeegga A**:

        - Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan: 100%
        - **Heerka**: **Heerka 1** (Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan > 96%)
        - **Qaddarka Qaybinta Ugu Badan**: 30,000 Jet

    - **Adeegga C**:

        - Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan: 66.67%
        - **Heerka**: **Heerka 4** (60% < Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan ≤ 77%)
        - **Qaddarka Qaybinta Ugu Badan**: 15,000 Jet

    - **Adeegga B**:

        - Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan: 33.33%
        - **Heerka**: **Heerka 6** (23% < Boqolkiiba Sare ee Urursan ≤ 40%)
        - **Qaddarka Qaybinta Ugu Badan**: 7,000 Jet

**Fiiro gaar ah**:

- **Dhibcaha Isku Midka ah**: Adeegyada leh dhibcaha waxqabadka isku midka ah waxay helayaan kala-saaris isku mid ah. Marka la xisaabinayo boqolkiiba sare ee urursan, celceliska kala-saarista ayaa la isticmaalaa.
- **Qaddarka Qaybinta Ugu Badan ee Token-ka** waxay tixraacdaa qaddarka ugu badan ee token-ka ee adeeggu heli karo iyadoo lagu saleynayo heerkiisa.

## Abaalmarinta Isticmaalaha iyo Isticmaalka Token-ka

### **Habka Abaalmarinta Isticmaalaha**

- **Abaalmarinta Ku Saleysan Hawlaha**: Isticmaalayaasha waxay helaan token-yada JetPack marka ay qabtaan **hawlo hore loo heshiiyay** (tusaale, abuurista nuxurka, ciyaarista) adeegga gudihiisa.
- **Shuruudaha Abaalmarinta Token-ka**: Qaddarka token-yada ee la abaalmariyo hawl kasta waxaa **go'aamiya wada-tashiga** u dhexeeya adeegga iyo hawl-wadeenka deegaanka JetPack.

### **Hababka Isticmaalka Token-ka**

1. **Gudaha Adeegga**:
    - **Helitaanka Astaamaha Premium**: Isticmaal token-yada si aad u hesho astaamaha ama nuxurka lacagta leh ee adeegga gudihiisa.
    - **Iibsashada Alaabta**: Iibso alaabta dhijitaalka ah ama alaabta adeegga gudihiisa adoo isticmaalaya token-yada.

2. **Isticmaalka Is-weydaarsiga Adeegga Jeebka JetPack**:
    - **Iibinta Token-ka**: Isticmaalayaasha waxay ku bedelan karaan token-yadooda **ADA** (lacagta **Cardano**).
    - **Maareynta Jeebka**: Jeebka JetPack wuxuu u oggolaanayaa maareynta amniga ee token-yada iyo wareejinta adeegyada kale.

### **Faa'iidooyinka La Filayo ee Ka Soo Baxay Qiimaha Token-ka oo Kordha**

- **Lacagta Token-ka**: Ka ganacsiga token-yada ee is-weydaarsiga waxay kordhisaa **lacagtooda**.
- **Faa'iidooyinka Dhaqaale**: Isticmaalayaasha waxay heli karaan **faa'iidooyin dhaqaale oo dheeri ah** iyagoo iibinaya token-yada haddii qiimahoodu kordho.

## Iibsashada iyo Dib-u-qaybinta Token-ka

### **Iibsashada Token-ka Dheeraadka ah ee Adeegyada**

- **Marka Token-yada la Qaybiyay ay Dhamaadaan**: Haddii adeeggu isticmaalo dhammaan token-yada loo qoondeeyay, wuxuu iibsan karaa token-yada dheeraadka ah ee **Is-weydaarsiga Adeegga Jeebka JetPack**.
- **Lacagta Iibsashada**: Token-yada waxaa lagu iibsadaa iyadoo la isticmaalayo **ADA**.
- **Isticmaalka Token-yada la Iibsaday**: Token-yada la iibsaday waxaa loo isticmaali karaa in lagu **abaalmariyo isticmaalayaasha** ama **gudaha adeegga**.

### **Token-yada Dib-u-iibsan kara iyo Token-yada aan Dib-u-iibsan karin**

- **Token-yada la Qaybiyay** (**Aan Dib-u-iibsan karin**): Token-yada loo qoondeeyay adeegyada **lama iibin karo** waxaana loo isticmaalaa oo kaliya **abaalmarinta isticmaalayaasha**.
- **Token-yada la Iibsaday iyo Token-yada laga Helay Isticmaalayaasha** (**Dib-u-iibsan kara**): Token-yada lagu iibsaday ADA iyo token-yada laga helay isticmaalayaasha **waa la iibin karaa**, taasoo ka caawinaysa **helitaanka lacagta**.

### **Dib-u-qaybinta Token-ka iyo Dhaqdhaqaaqa Deegaanka**

1. **Qaab-dhismeedka Wareegga Token-ka**:
    - Adeegyada waxay abaalmariyaan isticmaalayaasha token-yada; isticmaalayaashu waxay isticmaalaan gudaha adeegyada ama waxay ku iibiyaan is-weydaarsiga.
    - Adeegyada waxay helaan token-yada isticmaalayaasha ama waxay iibsadaan si ay u ilaaliyaan nidaam abaalmarin oo waara.

2. **Saamaynta Dhaqdhaqaaqa Deegaanka**:
    - Wareegga token-ka wuxuu dhiirigeliyaa **ka qaybgalka isticmaalayaasha** iyo **dhaqdhaqaaqa adeegga**.
    - Tani waxay ka qaybqaadataa **xasilinta qiimaha token-ka** iyo **koritaanka waara** ee deegaanka.
