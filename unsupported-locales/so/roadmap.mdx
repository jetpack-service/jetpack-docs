# Khariidadda Adeegga JetPack

Himilada Adeegga JetPack waa in la xaqiijiyo “**Wada noolaanshaha Isticmaalayaasha iyo Adeegyada**.”

**Wejiga 1** wuxuu qeexayaa astaamaha muhiimka ah iyo jadwalka dhammaystirka app-ka ee marxalad kasta, laga bilaabo Marxaladda-1 ilaa Marxaladda-5. Wejigan, dhammaan shaqooyinka lagama maarmaanka ah ee isku xirka aan kala go'a lahayn ee u dhexeeya nidaamka JetPack iyo adeegyadiisa ayaa la horumarin doonaa, iyadoo diiradda la saarayo soo saarista adeegyada muhiimka ah ee ku shaqeeya AI si tartiib tartiib ah. Marxalad kasta waxaa hagaya himilada **“Hindisaha Adeegga AI-First”**.

**Wejiga 2** wuxuu bilaaban doonaa marka Adeegga JetPack uu muujiyo qiime ku filan. Wejigan wuxuu soo bandhigi doonaa nidaamka xayaysiinta ee ku saleysan token-ka ee JetPack iyo isku xirka madal saddexaad sida metaverse iyo e-commerce. Jadwal faahfaahsan ayaa la shaacin doonaa goor dambe.


## Wejiga 1

### **Marxaladda-1: Marxaladda 'Gagarin'**
**Taariikhda Bilowga**: Janaayo 1, 2025

- Horumarinta nidaamka maaraynta adeegga
  - Shaqooyinka diiwaangelinta iyo ka saarista adeegga
  - Hirgelinta nidaamka qaybinta token-ka
- Horumarinta website-ka iyo API-ga aasaasiga ah ee REST

**Marxaladda Magaceeda**: Yuri Gagarin, qofkii ugu horreeyay ee aadanaha ah ee hawada sare tagay

---

### **Marxaladda-2: Marxaladda 'Shepard'**
**Jadwalka La Filayo**: Q3 2025

- Soo saarista app-ka **haru** ee xusuus-qorka
- Shaqada token-ka ee **OG Degen Bunny**
- Astaanta wareejinta token-ka JetPack ee u dhexeeya isticmaalayaasha
- Isku xirka taageerada boorsada dibadda

**Marxaladda Magaceeda**: Alan Shepard, qofkii ugu horreeyay ee Mareykan ah ee hawada sare tagay

---

### **Marxaladda-3: Marxaladda 'Tereshkova'**
**Jadwalka La Filayo**: Q1 2026

- Soo saarista **Adeegga Prompt**
- Horumarinta **Sarrifka Token-ka JetPack (CEX)**
- Horumarinta **Adeegga NFT Drop**
- Balaadhinta SDK luqado badan leh

**Marxaladda Magaceeda**: Valentina Tereshkova, haweeneydii ugu horreysay ee hawada sare tagtay

---

### **Marxaladda-4: Marxaladda 'Armstrong'**
**Jadwalka La Filayo**: Q3 2026

- Soo saarista adeegyada la xiriira NFT
- Horumarinta nidaamka codbixinta xeerarka ku saleysan **DAO**
  - Hirgelinta nidaamyada go'aan qaadashada ee baahsan

**Marxaladda Magaceeda**: Neil Armstrong, qofkii ugu horreeyay ee aadanaha ah ee cagta saaray dayaxa

---

### **Marxaladda-5: Marxaladda 'Leonov'**
**Jadwalka La Filayo**: Q1 2027

- Soo saarista adeegyada la xiriira **DeFi**
- Soo jiidashada bixiyeyaasha adeegga iyada oo loo marayo shirar dibadda ah
  - Dhageystayaasha la beegsanayo: ardayda jaamacadda, horumariyeyaasha kaligood shaqeeya, iwm.
- Horumarinta nidaamka kormeerka iyo ogaanshaha khiyaanada (FDS)
  - Xoojinta xasilloonida iyo amniga nidaamka

**Marxaladda Magaceeda**: Alexei Leonov, qofkii ugu horreeyay ee aadanaha ah ee sameeyay socod hawada sare ah
