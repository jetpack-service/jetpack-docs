# Yadda Tsarin Token na JetPack Yake Aiki

Tsarin token na JetPack ya ƙunshi **fitarwa da rarraba token bisa ayyuka**, **tsarin matakin sabis**, **lada ga masu amfani da hanyoyin amfani da token**, da kuma **tsarin saye da sake rarraba token**. Wannan tsari yana bayar da **tsarin lada mai adalci da inganci** ga masu amfani da masu samar da sabis.

## Tsarin Rarraba Token (Drop)

### Jimlar Adadin Token da Lokacin Rarraba

- **Jimlar Adadin**: JetPack zai fitar da jimlar **biliyan 1 na Jet** tokens.
- **Lokacin Rarraba**: Za a rarraba tokens a tsawon **shekaru 38**, tare da jimlar **rarrabawa 2,776** da za su faru a lokaci guda da **Cardano Epoch** (1 epoch = kwanaki 5).

### Adadin Rarraba Token a kowane Epoch

#### Lissafin Adadin Rarraba Bisa Ga Ayyukan Logarithmic

Adadin tokens da za a rarraba a kowane epoch an tsara shi ne don ya ragu a lokaci. Ana amfani da **ayyukan logarithmic** don lissafin adadin rarraba a kowane epoch. **1 Jet yana daidai da 1,000,000 Pack**, don haka ana lissafin adadin rarraba zuwa **wurin goma na shida** kuma ana biyan su daidai.

1. **Ma'anar Ayyukan Logarithmic $ f(t) $**:

   $$
   f(t) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

   Inda:

    - $ t $ shine lambar epoch, $ t = 1, 2, 3, \dotsc, 2776 $.
    - $ \ln $ shine aikin logarithm na halitta.

2. **Lissafin Jimlar Ayyukan Log $ f(t) $ A Duk Epochs**:

   Jimlar jimlar ƙimar aikin a duk lokacin rarraba $ S $ shine:

   $$
   S = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} f(t) = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

3. **Lissafin Kafaffen Scaling $ A $**:

   Jimlar adadin token na **biliyan 1 na Jet** an raba shi da jimlar $ S $ don daidaita adadin rarraba a kowane epoch:

   $$
   A = \dfrac{1,000,000,000}{S} = 2,412,005.7263929145
   $$

4. **Lissafin Adadin Rarraba Token a kowane Epoch**:

   Adadin rarraba a kowane epoch shine:

   $$
   \text{Distribution Amount}_t = A \times f(t) = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

5. **Misalan Adadin Rarraba**:

   Lissafin adadin rarraba don epochs na farko:

    - **Epoch 1**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_1 = 3,479,788.70 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(1 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 2**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_2 = 2,195,502.227 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 3**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_3 = 1,739,894.35 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(4)}
      $$

   Adadin rarraba a kowane epoch ana ƙayyade shi ne ta ƙimar aikin log da ya dace da lambar epoch.

**Halayen Rarraba**

- Tun da $ \ln(t + 1) $ yana ƙaruwa yayin da $ t $ ke ƙaruwa, mai rarraba yana zama babba, yana haifar da adadin rarraba gaba ɗaya ya ragu a hankali.
- Adadin rarraba a kowane epoch yana raguwa a lokaci, yana kusantar sifili.
- Wannan tsari yana ƙarfafa mahalarta na farko kuma yana ƙara ƙarancin token.

### Ka'idodin Rarraba Token a kowane Sabis

#### Lissafin Rabon Tsakanin Sabis

Rabon rarraba ga kowane sabis ana lissafa shi ne bisa ga ƙimar aikinsa $ P_n $ dangane da jimlar ƙimar aiki $ P $:

$$
\text{Ratio for Service } n = \frac{P_n}{P}
$$

Inda:

- $ P_n $: Ƙimar aiki na sabis $ n $.
- $ P $: Jimlar ƙimar aiki na duk sabis.

#### Lissafin Adadin Rarraba a kowane Sabis

Adadin tokens da sabis $ n $ ke karɓa a kowane epoch $ S_n $ shine:

$$
S_n = \min\left(f_{\text{total}}(t) \times \left( \frac{P_n}{P} \right), \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n\right)
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t) $: Daidaitaccen adadin rarraba gaba ɗaya don epoch.
- $ \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n $: Matsakaicin adadin token da aka ware bisa ga matakin sabis (duba matsakaicin rarraba token bisa matakin a ƙasa).

#### Sarrafa Tokens da Ba a Rarraba ba

##### Ɗaukar Tokens da Ba a Rarraba ba

- Tokens da ba a rarraba ba daga kowane epoch ana **ƙara** su zuwa adadin rarraba gaba ɗaya don epoch na gaba.
- Wannan yana tabbatar da ingantaccen rarraba token koda lokacin da halartar sabis na farko ya yi ƙasa.

##### Lissafin Daidaitaccen Adadin Rarraba Gaba ɗaya a kowane Epoch

Adadin rarraba gaba ɗaya don epoch na gaba shine:

$$
f_{\text{total}}(t+1) = f(t+1) + \text{Undistributed Tokens from Previous Epoch}
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t+1) $: Daidaitaccen adadin rarraba gaba ɗaya don epoch $ t+1 $.
- $ f(t+1) $: Adadin rarraba bisa ga aikin rarraba na asali.
- **Tokens da Ba a Rarraba ba da Aka Ɗauka**: Adadin tokens da ba a rarraba a epoch $ t $.

#### Lissafin Ƙimar Aiki ($ P_n $) a kowane Sabis

Ƙimar aiki na kowane sabis ana lissafa shi ne bisa ga waɗannan ma'auni. Ana iya daidaita nauyin kowane ma'auni ta hanyar gudanarwa.

1. **Cin Token na JetPack ($ M_{1n} $)**
    - **Nauyi**: $ w_1 = 50\% $
    - **Bayani**: Jimlar adadin tokens na JetPack da masu amfani suka cinye a cikin sabis.

2. **Adadin Kuri'un Degen Bunny ($ M_{2n} $)**
    - **Nauyi**: $ w_2 = 30\% $
    - **Bayani**: Adadin kuri'un da aka samu daga OG Degen Bunnies.

3. **Adadin Masu Amfani da Aiki ($ M_{3n} $)**
    - **Nauyi**: $ w_3 = 20\% $
    - **Bayani**: Adadin masu amfani na musamman da suka shiga kuma suka yi aiki a cikin sabis a lokacin epoch.

Ƙimar aiki na kowane sabis $ P_n $ ana lissafa shi ne:

$$
P_n = w_1 \times \left( \frac{M_{1n}}{M_{1\text{total}}} \right) + w_2 \times \left( \frac{M_{2n}}{M_{2\text{total}}} \right) + w_3 \times \left( \frac{M_{3n}}{M_{3\text{total}}} \right)
$$

Inda:

- $ M_{1n} $: Cin token na JetPack na sabis $ n $.
- $ M_{1\text{total}} $: Jimlar cin token na JetPack a duk sabis.
- $ M_{2n} $: Adadin kuri'un Degen Bunny na sabis $ n $.
- $ M_{2\text{total}} $: Jimlar kuri'un Degen Bunny a duk sabis.
- $ M_{3n} $: Adadin masu amfani da aiki na sabis $ n $.
- $ M_{3\text{total}} $: Jimlar masu amfani da aiki a duk sabis.

**Misali**:

- **Sabis A**:
    - $ M_{1A} = 500 $
    - $ M_{2A} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3A} = 200 $
- **Sabis B**:
    - $ M_{1B} = 300 $
    - $ M_{2B} = 200 $
    - $ M_{3B} = 100 $
- **Sabis C**:
    - $ M_{1C} = 200 $
    - $ M_{2C} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3C} = 700 $

**Jimloli**:

- $ M_{1\text{total}} = 500 + 300 + 200 = 1000 $
- $ M_{2\text{total}} = 150 + 200 + 150 = 500 $
- $ M_{3\text{total}} = 200 + 100 + 700 = 1000 $

**Nauyi**:

- $ w_1 = 0.5 $
- $ w_2 = 0.3 $
- $ w_3 = 0.2 $

**Lissafin Ƙimar Aiki na Sabis A**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_A &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{500}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.5 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.2 \\
&= 0.25 + 0.09 + 0.04 \\
&= 0.38
\end{align*}
$$

**Lissafin Ƙimar Aiki na Sabis B**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_B &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{300}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{200}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{100}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.3 + 0.3 \times 0.4 + 0.2 \times 0.1 \\
&= 0.15 + 0.12 + 0.02 \\
&= 0.29
\end{align*}
$$

**Lissafin Ƙimar Aiki na Sabis C**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_C &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{700}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.2 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.7 \\
&= 0.10 + 0.09 + 0.14 \\
&= 0.33
\end{align*}
$$

Don haka, ƙimar aikin su shine:

- **Sabis A**: $ P_A = 0.38 $
- **Sabis B**: $ P_B = 0.29 $
- **Sabis C**: $ P_C = 0.33 $

##### Hanyar Ƙayyade Mataki

1. **Lissafin Ƙimar Aiki**

   Yi amfani da hanyar da aka bayyana a baya don lissafin ƙimar aiki na kowane sabis $ P_n $.

2. **Ƙayyade Matsayin Sabis**

   Yi jerin duk sabis a **tsari mai sauƙi** bisa ga ƙimar aikin su.

3. **Lissafin Cumulative Top Percentiles**

   Don kowane sabis, lissafa cumulative top percentile:

   $$
   \text{Cumulative Top Percentile (\%)} = \left(1 - \frac{\text{Rank} - 1}{N} \right) \times 100\%
   $$

   Inda:

    - $ \text{Rank} $: Matsayin sabis (fara daga 1).
    - $ N $: Jimlar adadin sabis.

4. **Rarraba Matakai**

   Rarraba matakai bisa ga cumulative top percentiles:

    - **Mataki 1**: Cumulative top percentile > 96%
    - **Mataki 2**: 89% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 96%
    - **Mataki 3**: 77% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 89%
    - **Mataki 4**: 60% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 77%
    - **Mataki 5**: 40% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 60%
    - **Mataki 6**: 23% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 40%
    - **Mataki 7**: 11% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 23%
    - **Mataki 8**: 4% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 11%
    - **Mataki 9**: Cumulative top percentile ≤ 4%

### Tasirin Tokens da Ba a Rarraba ba da Aka Tara

- **Ƙara Halartar Sabis da Ingantaccen Aiki** zai rage adadin tokens da ba a rarraba ba a hankali.
- **Sabis na farko na iya amfani da matsakaicin adadin rarraba** don haɓaka ci gaba.
- Ɗaukar tokens yana taimakawa **tsare darajar** da kuma daidaita yawan juyawa.

## Lada ga Masu Amfani da Amfani da Token

### **Tsarin Lada ga Masu Amfani**

- **Lada Bisa Ayyuka**: Masu amfani suna karɓar tokens na JetPack lokacin da suka yi **ayyuka da aka amince da su** (misali, ƙirƙirar abun ciki, wasa) a cikin sabis.
- **Ka'idodin Lada Token**: Adadin tokens da za a bayar don kowane aiki ana **ƙayyade shi ta hanyar tattaunawa** tsakanin sabis da mai gudanar da tsarin JetPack.

### **Hanyoyin Amfani da Token**

1. **A Cikin Sabis**:
    - **Samun Abubuwan Farko**: Yi amfani da tokens don samun abubuwan da aka biya ko abun ciki a cikin sabis.
    - **Sayi Kayayyaki**: Sayi kayayyakin dijital ko abubuwan sabis ta amfani da tokens.

2. **Amfani da Musayar Sabis na JetPack Wallet**:
    - **Sayen Token**: Masu amfani na iya musanya tokens ɗin su don **ADA** (kuɗin **Cardano**).
    - **Gudanar da Wallet**: JetPack wallet yana ba da damar sarrafa tokens cikin aminci da canja wurin su zuwa wasu sabis.

### **Fa'idodin da Ake Sa Ran Samu Daga Ƙimar Token**

- **Liquidity na Token**: Kasuwancin tokens a kan musayar yana ƙara **liquidity** ɗin su.
- **Ribobi na Tattalin Arziki**: Masu amfani na iya samun **ƙarin ribobi na tattalin arziki** ta hanyar sayar da tokens idan ƙimar su ta ƙaru.

## Saye da Sake Rarraba Token

### **Saye na Ƙarin Token ta Sabis**

- **Lokacin da Aka Ƙare da Tokens da Aka Rarraba**: Idan sabis ya yi amfani da duk tokens da aka ware masa, zai iya sayen ƙarin tokens daga **Musayar Sabis na JetPack Wallet**.
- **Kuɗin Saye**: Ana sayen tokens ta amfani da **ADA**.
- **Amfani da Tokens da Aka Saye**: Tokens da aka saya za a iya amfani da su don **lada ga masu amfani** ko **a cikin sabis**.

### **Tokens da Za a Iya Sake Sayarwa da Waɗanda Ba Za a Iya Sake Sayarwa ba**

- **Tokens da Aka Rarraba** (**Ba Za a Iya Sake Sayarwa ba**): Tokens da aka ware wa sabis **ba za a iya sake sayar da su ba** kuma suna ne kawai don **lada ga masu amfani**.
- **Tokens da Aka Saye da Tokens da Aka Samu daga Masu Amfani** (**Za a Iya Sake Sayarwa**): Tokens da aka saya da ADA da tokens da aka samu daga masu amfani **za a iya sake sayar da su**, suna taimakawa wajen **samuwa liquidity**.

### **Sake Rarraba Token da Ƙaddamar da Tsarin**

1. **Tsarin Juyawa na Token**:
    - Sabis suna ba da lada ga masu amfani da tokens; masu amfani suna amfani da su a cikin sabis ko sayar da su a kan musayar.
    - Sabis suna samun tokens daga masu amfani ko sayen su don kula da **tsarin lada mai dorewa**.

2. **Tasirin Ƙaddamar da Tsarin**:
    - Juyawar token yana haɓaka **halartar masu amfani** da **ƙaddamar da sabis**.
    - Wannan yana taimakawa wajen **tsare ƙimar token** da **ci gaban tsarin mai dorewa**.
