# Taswirar Aikin JetPack

Manufar Aikin JetPack ita ce cimma “**Zama Tare Tsakanin Masu Amfani da Ayyuka**.”

**Mataki na 1** yana bayyana mahimman fasaloli da jadawalin kammala manhaja a kowane mataki, daga Mataki-1 zuwa Mataki-5. A wannan matakin, za a haɓaka dukkan ayyukan da ake buƙata don haɗin kai mai sauƙi tsakanin tsarin JetPack da ayyukansa, tare da mayar da hankali kan ƙaddamar da ainihin ayyukan AI a hankali. Kowanne mataki yana jagorantar da manufar **“Shirin Aikin AI-Na Farko”**.

**Mataki na 2** zai fara da zarar Aikin JetPack ya nuna isasshen daraja. Wannan matakin zai gabatar da tsarin tallan JetPack na token da haɗin kai tare da dandamali na ɓangare na uku kamar metaverse da e-commerce. Za a sanar da cikakken jadawali daga baya.


## Mataki na 1

### **Mataki-1: Matakin 'Gagarin'**
**Ranar Fara**: Janairu 1, 2025

- Haɓaka tsarin gudanar da ayyuka
  - Ayyukan rajista da cire ayyuka
  - Aiwatar da tsarin rarraba token
- Haɓaka gidan yanar gizo da asali REST API

**Mataki An Sanya Suna Bayan**: Yuri Gagarin, mutum na farko a sararin samaniya

---

### **Mataki-2: Matakin 'Shepard'**
**Jadawalin Da Ake Sa Ran**: Q3 2025

- Kaddamar da manhajar **haru** diary
- Ayyukan saukar da token na **OG Degen Bunny**
- Fasalin canja wurin token na JetPack tsakanin masu amfani
- Haɗin kai na tallafin walat na waje

**Mataki An Sanya Suna Bayan**: Alan Shepard, Ba'amurke na farko a sararin samaniya

---

### **Mataki-3: Matakin 'Tereshkova'**
**Jadawalin Da Ake Sa Ran**: Q1 2026

- Kaddamar da **Aikin Prompt**
- Haɓaka **JetPack Token Exchange (CEX)**
- Haɓaka **Aikin Saukar da NFT**
- Faɗaɗa SDK mai harsuna da yawa

**Mataki An Sanya Suna Bayan**: Valentina Tereshkova, mace ta farko a sararin samaniya

---

### **Mataki-4: Matakin 'Armstrong'**
**Jadawalin Da Ake Sa Ran**: Q3 2026

- Kaddamar da ayyuka masu alaƙa da NFT
- Haɓaka tsarin zaɓen dokoki na **DAO**
  - Aiwatar da tsarin yanke shawara na rarraba

**Mataki An Sanya Suna Bayan**: Neil Armstrong, mutum na farko da ya taka ƙafa a wata

---

### **Mataki-5: Matakin 'Leonov'**
**Jadawalin Da Ake Sa Ran**: Q1 2027

- Kaddamar da ayyuka masu alaƙa da **DeFi**
- Jawo masu ba da sabis ta hanyar tarurrukan waje
  - Masu sauraro da aka nufa: ɗaliban jami'a, masu haɓaka kai, da sauransu
- Inganta tsarin sa ido da gano zamba (FDS)
  - Ƙarfafa kwanciyar hankali da tsaro na tsarin

**Mataki An Sanya Suna Bayan**: Alexei Leonov, mutum na farko da ya yi tafiya a sararin samaniya
