### Kuchuluka kwa Ma Token Ogawidwa pa Epoch

#### Kuwerengera Kuchuluka kwa Kugawa Kutengera Ntchito ya Logarithmic

Kuchuluka kwa ma token ogawidwa pa epoch kumapangidwa kuti kuchepe pakapita nthawi. **Ntchito ya logarithmic** imagwiritsidwa ntchito kuwerengera kuchuluka kwa kugawa kwa epoch iliyonse. **1 Jet ndi yofanana ndi 1,000,000 Pack**, kotero kuchuluka kwa kugawa kumawerengedwa mpaka **malo asanu ndi limodzi a decimal** ndikulipidwa moyenera.

1. **Kufotokozera Ntchito ya Logarithmic $ f(t) $**:

   $$
   f(t) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

   Kumene:

    - $ t $ ndi nambala ya epoch, $ t = 1, 2, 3, \dotsc, 2776 $.
    - $ \ln $ ndi ntchito ya logarithm yachilengedwe.

2. **Kuwerengera Chiwerengero cha Ntchito ya Log $ f(t) $ pa Epoch Zonse**:

   Chiwerengero chonse cha mtengo wa ntchito pa nthawi yonse yogawa $ S $ ndi:

   $$
   S = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} f(t) = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

3. **Kuwerengera Chokhazikika cha Scaling $ A $**:

   Chiwerengero chonse cha ma token **1 biliyoni Jet** chimagawidwa ndi chiwerengero $ S $ kuti chikonze kuchuluka kwa kugawa kwa epoch iliyonse:

   $$
   A = \dfrac{1,000,000,000}{S} = 2,412,005.7263929145
   $$

4. **Kuwerengera Kuchuluka kwa Kugawa kwa Ma Token pa Epoch**:

   Kuchuluka kwa kugawa kwa epoch iliyonse ndi:

   $$
   \text{Distribution Amount}_t = A \times f(t) = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

5. **Zitsanzo za Kuchuluka kwa Kugawa**:

   Kuwerengera kuchuluka kwa kugawa kwa ma epoch oyambilira:

    - **Epoch 1**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_1 = 3,479,788.70 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(1 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 2**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_2 = 2,195,502.227 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 3**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_3 = 1,739,894.35 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(4)}
      $$

   Kuchuluka kwa kugawa kwa epoch iliyonse kumatsimikiziridwa ndi mtengo wa ntchito ya log yomwe ikugwirizana ndi nambala ya epoch.

**Makhalidwe a Kugawa**

- Popeza $ \ln(t + 1) $ imakwera pamene $ t $ imakwera, chiwerengero chimakhala chachikulu, zomwe zimapangitsa kuti kuchuluka kwa kugawa kuchepe pang'onopang'ono.
- Kuchuluka kwa kugawa pa epoch kumachepa pakapita nthawi, kufika pafupi ndi ziro.
- Kapangidwe kameneka kamalimbikitsa omwe akutenga nawo mbali koyambirira ndikuwonjezera kusowa kwa ma token.

### Zofunikira pa Kugawa Ma Token pa Ntchito

#### Kuwerengera Ma Ratio Pakati pa Ntchito

Chiwerengero cha kugawa kwa ntchito iliyonse chimawerengedwa kutengera chiwerengero cha magwiridwe ake $ P_n $ poyerekeza ndi chiwerengero cha magwiridwe onse $ P $:

$$
\text{Ratio for Service } n = \frac{P_n}{P}
$$

Kumene:

- $ P_n $: Chiwerengero cha magwiridwe a ntchito $ n $.
- $ P $: Chiwerengero cha magwiridwe a ntchito zonse.

#### Kuwerengera Kuchuluka kwa Kugawa pa Ntchito

Kuchuluka kwa ma token omwe ntchito $ n $ imalandira pa epoch iliyonse $ S_n $ ndi:

$$
S_n = \min\left(f_{\text{total}}(t) \times \left( \frac{P_n}{P} \right), \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n\right)
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t) $: Kuchuluka kwa kugawa konse komwe kwakonzedwa kwa epoch.
- $ \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n $: Kuchuluka kwa ma token omwe agawidwa kutengera mlingo wa ntchito (onani kugawa kwa ma token kutengera mlingo pansipa).

#### Kusamalira Ma Token Osagawidwa

##### Kusunga Ma Token Osagawidwa

- Ma token osagawidwa kuchokera pa epoch iliyonse **awonjezedwa** ku kuchuluka kwa kugawa konse kwa epoch yotsatira.
- Izi zimatsimikizira kugawa ma token moyenera ngakhale pamene kutenga nawo mbali kwa ntchito koyambirira kuli kochepa.

##### Kuwerengera Kuchuluka kwa Kugawa Konse Komwe Kwakonzedwa pa Epoch

Kuchuluka kwa kugawa konse kwa epoch yotsatira ndi:

$$
f_{\text{total}}(t+1) = f(t+1) + \text{Undistributed Tokens from Previous Epoch}
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t+1) $: Kuchuluka kwa kugawa konse komwe kwakonzedwa kwa epoch $ t+1 $.
- $ f(t+1) $: Kuchuluka kwa kugawa kutengera ntchito yoyambira.
- **Ma Token Osagawidwa Osungidwa**: Chiwerengero cha ma token omwe sanagawidwe pa epoch $ t $.

#### Kuwerengera Chiwerengero cha Magwiridwe ($ P_n $) pa Ntchito

Chiwerengero cha magwiridwe a ntchito iliyonse chimawerengedwa kutengera zotsatirazi. Kulemera kwa chizindikiro chilichonse kumatha kusinthidwa kudzera mu kasamalidwe.

1. **Kugwiritsa Ntchito Ma Token a JetPack ($ M_{1n} $)**
    - **Kulemera**: $ w_1 = 50\% $
    - **Kufotokozera**: Chiwerengero chonse cha ma token a JetPack omwe agwiritsidwa ntchito ndi ogwiritsa ntchito mkati mwa ntchito.

2. **Chiwerengero cha Mavoti a Degen Bunny ($ M_{2n} $)**
    - **Kulemera**: $ w_2 = 30\% $
    - **Kufotokozera**: Chiwerengero cha mavoti omwe alandilidwa kuchokera ku OG Degen Bunnies.

3. **Chiwerengero cha Ogwiritsa Ntchito Ogwira Ntchito ($ M_{3n} $)**
    - **Kulemera**: $ w_3 = 20\% $
    - **Kufotokozera**: Chiwerengero cha ogwiritsa ntchito apadera omwe adalowa ndikugwira ntchito mu ntchito pa epoch.

Chiwerengero cha magwiridwe a ntchito iliyonse $ P_n $ chimawerengedwa motere:

$$
P_n = w_1 \times \left( \frac{M_{1n}}{M_{1\text{total}}} \right) + w_2 \times \left( \frac{M_{2n}}{M_{2\text{total}}} \right) + w_3 \times \left( \frac{M_{3n}}{M_{3\text{total}}} \right)
$$

Kumene:

- $ M_{1n} $: Kugwiritsa ntchito ma token a JetPack kwa ntchito $ n $.
- $ M_{1\text{total}} $: Kugwiritsa ntchito ma token a JetPack konse pa ntchito zonse.
- $ M_{2n} $: Chiwerengero cha mavoti a Degen Bunny kwa ntchito $ n $.
- $ M_{2\text{total}} $: Chiwerengero cha mavoti a Degen Bunny konse pa ntchito zonse.
- $ M_{3n} $: Chiwerengero cha ogwiritsa ntchito ogwira ntchito kwa ntchito $ n $.
- $ M_{3\text{total}} $: Chiwerengero cha ogwiritsa ntchito ogwira ntchito konse pa ntchito zonse.

**Chitsanzo**:

- **Ntchito A**:
    - $ M_{1A} = 500 $
    - $ M_{2A} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3A} = 200 $
- **Ntchito B**:
    - $ M_{1B} = 300 $
    - $ M_{2B} = 200 $
    - $ M_{3B} = 100 $
- **Ntchito C**:
    - $ M_{1C} = 200 $
    - $ M_{2C} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3C} = 700 $

**Chiwerengero Chonse**:

- $ M_{1\text{total}} = 500 + 300 + 200 = 1000 $
- $ M_{2\text{total}} = 150 + 200 + 150 = 500 $
- $ M_{3\text{total}} = 200 + 100 + 700 = 1000 $

**Kulemera**:

- $ w_1 = 0.5 $
- $ w_2 = 0.3 $
- $ w_3 = 0.2 $

**Kuwerengera Chiwerengero cha Magwiridwe a Ntchito A**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_A &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{500}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.5 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.2 \\
&= 0.25 + 0.09 + 0.04 \\
&= 0.38
\end{align*}
$$

**Kuwerengera Chiwerengero cha Magwiridwe a Ntchito B**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_B &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{300}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{200}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{100}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.3 + 0.3 \times 0.4 + 0.2 \times 0.1 \\
&= 0.15 + 0.12 + 0.02 \\
&= 0.29
\end{align*}
$$

**Kuwerengera Chiwerengero cha Magwiridwe a Ntchito C**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_C &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{700}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.2 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.7 \\
&= 0.10 + 0.09 + 0.14 \\
&= 0.33
\end{align*}
$$

Choncho, mawerengero a magwiridwe ndi awa:

- **Ntchito A**: $ P_A = 0.38 $
- **Ntchito B**: $ P_B = 0.29 $
- **Ntchito C**: $ P_C = 0.33 $

## Mphoto za Ogwiritsa Ntchito ndi Kugwiritsa Ntchito Ma Token

### **Njira ya Mphoto za Ogwiritsa Ntchito**

- **Mphoto Zotengera Zochitika**: Ogwiritsa ntchito amalandira ma token a JetPack akachita **zochitika zomwe zagonjetsedwa** (mwachitsanzo, kupanga zinthu, kusewera masewera) mkati mwa ntchito.
- **Zofunikira pa Mphoto za Ma Token**: Kuchuluka kwa ma token omwe amaperekedwa pa zochitika zilizonse **kumatsimikiziridwa kudzera mu kukambirana** pakati pa ntchito ndi woyendetsa chilengedwe cha JetPack.

### **Njira Zogwiritsira Ntchito Ma Token**

1. **Mkati mwa Ntchito**:
    - **Kufikira Zinthu Zokwera Mtengo**: Gwiritsani ntchito ma token kuti mufikire zinthu zolipidwa kapena zomwe zili mkati mwa ntchito.
    - **Kugula Zinthu**: Gulani zinthu zamagetsi kapena zinthu zomwe zili mu ntchito pogwiritsa ntchito ma token.

2. **Kugwiritsa Ntchito Kusinthanitsa kwa JetPack Wallet Service**:
    - **Kugulitsa Ma Token**: Ogwiritsa ntchito amatha kusinthanitsa ma token awo ndi **ADA** (ndalama ya **Cardano**).
    - **Kusamalira Wallet**: JetPack wallet imalola kasamalidwe kotetezeka ka ma token ndi kusamutsa ku ntchito zina.

### **Ubwino Woyembekezeka kuchokera ku Kukwera Mtengo kwa Ma Token**

- **Kuyenda kwa Ma Token**: Kusinthanitsa ma token pa misika kumawonjezera **kuyenda kwawo**.
- **Phindu Lachuma**: Ogwiritsa ntchito amatha kupeza **phindu lachuma lowonjezera** pogulitsa ma token ngati mtengo wawo ukwera.

- **Ndalama Zogulira**: Ma token amagulidwa pogwiritsa ntchito **ADA**.
- **Kugwiritsa Ntchito Ma Token Ogulidwa**: Ma token ogulidwa amatha kugwiritsidwa ntchito **kupereka mphoto kwa ogwiritsa ntchito** kapena **mkati mwa ntchito**.

### **Ma Token Ogulitsidwa ndi Osagulitsidwa**

- **Ma Token Ogawidwa** (**Osagulitsidwa**): Ma token omwe agawidwa ku ntchito **sangagulitsidwe** ndipo ndi okha **kwa mphoto za ogwiritsa ntchito**.
- **Ma Token Ogulidwa ndi Ma Token Opezeka kwa Ogwiritsa Ntchito** (**Ogulitsidwa**): Ma token ogulidwa ndi ADA ndi ma token omwe alandilidwa kuchokera kwa ogwiritsa ntchito **angagulitsidwe**, kuthandiza **kupeza kuyenda**.

### **Kugawa Ma Token ndi Kuyambitsa Chilengedwe**

1. **Kapangidwe ka Kuyenda kwa Ma Token**:
    - Ntchito zimapereka mphoto kwa ogwiritsa ntchito ndi ma token; ogwiritsa ntchito amagwiritsa ntchito mkati mwa ntchito kapena kugulitsa pa misika.
    - Ntchito zimapeza ma token kuchokera kwa ogwiritsa ntchito kapena kugula kuti zisunge **dongosolo la mphoto lokhazikika**.

2. **Zotsatira za Kuyambitsa Chilengedwe**:
    - Kuyenda kwa ma token kumalimbikitsa **kutenga nawo mbali kwa ogwiritsa ntchito** ndi **kuyambitsa ntchito**.
    - Izi zimathandizira **kukhazikika kwa mtengo wa ma token** ndi **kukula kokhazikika** kwa chilengedwe.
