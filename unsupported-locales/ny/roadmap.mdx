# JetPack Service Roadmap

Masomphenya a JetPack Service ndi kukwaniritsa “**Kukhala Pamodzi Pakati pa Ogwiritsa Ntchito ndi Ntchito**.”

**Gawo 1** limafotokoza mbali zofunika ndi nthawi yomaliza ya mapulogalamu pa siteji iliyonse, kuyambira Stage-1 mpaka Stage-5. M'gawo ili, ntchito zonse zofunika kuti JetPack system ndi ntchito zake zigwirizane mosavuta zidzapangidwa, ndikuyang'ana kwambiri kukhazikitsa ntchito zazikulu zoyendetsedwa ndi AI pang'onopang'ono. Gawo lililonse limatsogozedwa ndi cholinga cha **“AI-First Service Initiative”**.

**Gawo 2** lidzayamba JetPack Service ikawonetsa phindu lokwanira. Gawo ili lidzayambitsa dongosolo la malonda la JetPack lokhala ndi ma tokeni ndi kulumikizana ndi nsanja za ena monga metaverse ndi e-commerce. Ndandanda yatsatanetsatane idzalengezedwa mtsogolo.


## Gawo 1

### **Stage-1: 'Gagarin' Stage**
**Tsiku Loyambira**: Januware 1, 2025

- Kukula kwa dongosolo loyang'anira ntchito
  - Ntchito zolembetsa ndi kuchotsa ntchito
  - Kukwaniritsidwa kwa dongosolo la kugawa ma tokeni
- Kukula kwa webusaiti ndi basic REST API

**Stage Yotchedwa Mwa**: Yuri Gagarin, munthu woyamba kupita kumlengalenga

---

### **Stage-2: 'Shepard' Stage**
**Nthawi Yoyembekezera**: Q3 2025

- Kukhazikitsidwa kwa pulogalamu ya **haru** diary
- Ntchito ya **OG Degen Bunny** token drop
- Mbali ya JetPack token transfer pakati pa ogwiritsa ntchito
- Kulumikizana ndi thandizo la chikwama chakunja

**Stage Yotchedwa Mwa**: Alan Shepard, woyamba wa ku America kupita kumlengalenga

---

### **Stage-3: 'Tereshkova' Stage**
**Nthawi Yoyembekezera**: Q1 2026

- Kukhazikitsidwa kwa **Prompt Service**
- Kukula kwa **JetPack Token Exchange (CEX)**
- Kukula kwa **NFT Drop Service**
- Kukulitsa kwa multilingual SDK

**Stage Yotchedwa Mwa**: Valentina Tereshkova, mkazi woyamba kupita kumlengalenga

---

### **Stage-4: 'Armstrong' Stage**
**Nthawi Yoyembekezera**: Q3 2026

- Kukhazikitsidwa kwa ntchito zokhudzana ndi NFT
- Kukula kwa **DAO-based rules voting system**
  - Kukwaniritsidwa kwa dongosolo la zisankho lodziyimira pawokha

**Stage Yotchedwa Mwa**: Neil Armstrong, munthu woyamba kuyenda pa mwezi

---

### **Stage-5: 'Leonov' Stage**
**Nthawi Yoyembekezera**: Q1 2027

- Kukhazikitsidwa kwa ntchito zokhudzana ndi DeFi
- Kukopa opereka ntchito kudzera m'misonkhano yakunja
  - Omvera omwe akufuna: ophunzira aku yunivesite, opanga okha, etc.
- Kukulitsa kwa dongosolo loyang'anira ndi kuzindikira chinyengo (FDS)
  - Kulimbikitsa kukhazikika ndi chitetezo cha dongosolo

**Stage Yotchedwa Mwa**: Alexei Leonov, munthu woyamba kuchita spacewalk
