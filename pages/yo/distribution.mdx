# Bawo ni Awọn Tokeni JetPack Ṣe N ṣiṣẹ

Eto tokeni JetPack ni **ifisilẹ ati pinpin tokeni ti o da lori iṣẹ-ṣiṣe**, eto **ipele iṣẹ**, **awọn ere olumulo ati awọn ọna lilo tokeni**, ati **ẹrọ fun rira ati pinpin awọn tokeni**. Eto yii pese **eto ere ti o tọ ati ti o munadoko** fun awọn olumulo ati awọn olupese iṣẹ mejeeji.

## Ẹrọ Pinpin Tokeni (Drop)

### Lapapọ Ipese Tokeni ati Akoko Pinpin

- **Lapapọ Ipese**: JetPack yoo funni ni apapọ ti **1 bilionu Jet** awọn tokeni.
- **Akoko Pinpin**: Awọn tokeni yoo pinpin lori **ọdun 38**, pẹlu apapọ ti **2,776 pinpin** ti o waye ni awọn akoko kanna bi **Cardano Epoch** (1 epoch = ọjọ 5).

### Iye Pinpin Tokeni fun Epoch

#### Ṣiṣiro Iye Pinpin Da lori Iṣẹ Logarithmic

Iye awọn tokeni ti a pinpin fun epoch jẹ apẹrẹ lati dinku lori akoko. A **iṣẹ logarithmic** ni a lo lati ṣe iṣiro iye pinpin fun epoch kọọkan. **1 Jet jẹ deede si 1,000,000 Pack**, nitorinaa awọn iye pinpin ni a ṣe iṣiro si **ibi kẹfa ti desimali** ati sanwo ni ibamu.

1. **Itumọ ti Iṣẹ Logarithmic $ f(t) $**:

   $$
   f(t) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

   Nibo ni:

    - $ t $ jẹ nọmba epoch, $ t = 1, 2, 3, \dotsc, 2776 $.
    - $ \ln $ jẹ iṣẹ logarithm adayeba.

2. **Ṣiṣiro Lapapọ Iṣẹ Log $ f(t) $ Lori Gbogbo Awọn Epochs**:

   Lapapọ iye awọn iye iṣẹ lori gbogbo akoko pinpin $ S $ jẹ:

   $$
   S = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} f(t) = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

3. **Ṣiṣiro Iwọn Aṣa $ A $**:

   Lapapọ ipese tokeni ti **1 bilionu Jet** ti pin nipasẹ apapọ $ S $ lati ṣatunṣe iye pinpin fun epoch kọọkan:

   $$
   A = \dfrac{1,000,000,000}{S} = 2,412,005.7263929145
   $$

4. **Ṣiṣiro Iye Pinpin Tokeni fun Epoch**:

   Iye pinpin fun epoch kọọkan jẹ:

   $$
   \text{Distribution Amount}_t = A \times f(t) = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

5. **Awọn Apẹẹrẹ ti Awọn Iye Pinpin**:

   Ṣiṣiro awọn iye pinpin fun awọn epoch ibẹrẹ:

    - **Epoch 1**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_1 = 3,479,788.70 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(1 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 2**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_2 = 2,195,502.227 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 3**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_3 = 1,739,894.35 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(4)}
      $$

   Iye pinpin fun epoch kọọkan jẹ ipinnu nipasẹ iye iṣẹ log ti o baamu si nọmba epoch.

**Awọn abuda Pinpin**

- Niwọn igba ti $ \ln(t + 1) $ n pọ si bi $ t $ n pọ si, denominator di nla, ti o fa ki iye pinpin lapapọ dinku ni kẹkẹ.
- Iye pinpin fun epoch kọọkan dinku lori akoko, sunmọ odo.
- Apẹrẹ yii ṣe iwuri fun awọn olukopa ibẹrẹ ati mu ainiye tokeni pọ si.

### Awọn Ilana Pinpin Tokeni fun Iṣẹ

#### Ṣiṣiro Awọn ipin Laarin Awọn Iṣẹ

Ipin pinpin fun iṣẹ kọọkan ni a ṣe iṣiro da lori iwọn iṣẹ rẹ $ P_n $ ni ibatan si iwọn iṣẹ lapapọ $ P $:

$$
\text{Ratio for Service } n = \frac{P_n}{P}
$$

Nibo ni:

- $ P_n $: Iwọn iṣẹ ti iṣẹ $ n $.
- $ P $: Lapapọ awọn iwọn iṣẹ ti gbogbo awọn iṣẹ.

#### Ṣiṣiro Iye Pinpin fun Iṣẹ

Iye awọn tokeni ti iṣẹ $ n $ gba ni epoch kọọkan $ S_n $ jẹ:

$$
S_n = \min\left(f_{\text{total}}(t) \times \left( \frac{P_n}{P} \right), \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n\right)
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t) $: Iye pinpin lapapọ ti a ṣatunṣe fun epoch.
- $ \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n $: Iye tokeni ti o pọju ti a pin da lori ipele iṣẹ (wo pinpin tokeni ti o pọju ti o da lori ipele ni isalẹ).

#### Iṣakoso ti Awọn Tokeni ti a ko Pinpin

##### Gbigbe Awọn Tokeni ti a ko Pinpin

- Awọn tokeni ti a ko pinpin lati epoch kọọkan ni a **fi kun** si iye pinpin lapapọ fun epoch ti n bọ.
- Eyi ṣe idaniloju pinpin tokeni ti o munadoko paapaa nigbati ikopa iṣẹ ibẹrẹ ba kere.

##### Ṣiṣiro Iye Pinpin Lapapọ ti a Ṣatunṣe fun Epoch

Iye pinpin lapapọ fun epoch ti n bọ jẹ:

$$
f_{\text{total}}(t+1) = f(t+1) + \text{Undistributed Tokens from Previous Epoch}
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t+1) $: Iye pinpin lapapọ ti a ṣatunṣe fun epoch $ t+1 $.
- $ f(t+1) $: Iye pinpin ti o da lori iṣẹ pinpin ipilẹ.
- **Awọn Tokeni ti a ko Pinpin ti a gbe lọ**: Nọmba awọn tokeni ti a ko pinpin ni epoch $ t $.

#### Ṣiṣiro Iwọn Iṣẹ ($ P_n $) fun Iṣẹ

Iwọn iṣẹ ti iṣẹ kọọkan ni a ṣe iṣiro da lori awọn metiriki wọnyi. Iwọn ti ọkọọkan metiriki le ṣe atunṣe nipasẹ iṣakoso.

1. **Iṣamulo Tokeni JetPack ($ M_{1n} $)**
    - **Iwọn**: $ w_1 = 50\% $
    - **Apejuwe**: Lapapọ iye awọn tokeni JetPack ti awọn olumulo lo laarin iṣẹ naa.

2. **Degen Bunny Vote Count ($ M_{2n} $)**
    - **Iwọn**: $ w_2 = 30\% $
    - **Apejuwe**: Nọmba awọn ibo ti a gba lati OG Degen Bunnies.

3. **Active User Count ($ M_{3n} $)**
    - **Iwọn**: $ w_3 = 20\% $
    - **Apejuwe**: Nọmba awọn olumulo alailẹgbẹ ti o wọle ati ti nṣiṣe lọwọ ninu iṣẹ lakoko epoch naa.

Iwọn iṣẹ ti iṣẹ kọọkan $ P_n $ ni a ṣe iṣiro bi:

$$
P_n = w_1 \times \left( \frac{M_{1n}}{M_{1\text{total}}} \right) + w_2 \times \left( \frac{M_{2n}}{M_{2\text{total}}} \right) + w_3 \times \left( \frac{M_{3n}}{M_{3\text{total}}} \right)
$$

Nibo ni:

- $ M_{1n} $: Iṣamulo tokeni JetPack ti iṣẹ $ n $.
- $ M_{1\text{total}} $: Lapapọ iṣamulo tokeni JetPack kọja gbogbo awọn iṣẹ.
- $ M_{2n} $: Degen Bunny vote count fun iṣẹ $ n $.
- $ M_{2\text{total}} $: Lapapọ awọn ibo Degen Bunny kọja gbogbo awọn iṣẹ.
- $ M_{3n} $: Active user count ti iṣẹ $ n $.
- $ M_{3\text{total}} $: Lapapọ awọn olumulo ti nṣiṣe lọwọ kọja gbogbo awọn iṣẹ.

**Apeere**:

- **Iṣẹ A**:
    - $ M_{1A} = 500 $
    - $ M_{2A} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3A} = 200 $
- **Iṣẹ B**:
    - $ M_{1B} = 300 $
    - $ M_{2B} = 200 $
    - $ M_{3B} = 100 $
- **Iṣẹ C**:
    - $ M_{1C} = 200 $
    - $ M_{2C} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3C} = 700 $

**Lapapọ**:

- $ M_{1\text{total}} = 500 + 300 + 200 = 1000 $
- $ M_{2\text{total}} = 150 + 200 + 150 = 500 $
- $ M_{3\text{total}} = 200 + 100 + 700 = 1000 $

**Awọn Iwọn**:

- $ w_1 = 0.5 $
- $ w_2 = 0.3 $
- $ w_3 = 0.2 $

**Ṣiṣiro Iwọn Iṣẹ fun Iṣẹ A**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_A &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{500}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.5 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.2 \\
&= 0.25 + 0.09 + 0.04 \\
&= 0.38
\end{align*}
$$

**Ṣiṣiro Iwọn Iṣẹ fun Iṣẹ B**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_B &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{300}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{200}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{100}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.3 + 0.3 \times 0.4 + 0.2 \times 0.1 \\
&= 0.15 + 0.12 + 0.02 \\
&= 0.29
\end{align*}
$$

**Ṣiṣiro Iwọn Iṣẹ fun Iṣẹ C**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_C &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{700}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.2 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.7 \\
&= 0.10 + 0.09 + 0.14 \\
&= 0.33
\end{align*}
$$

Nitorinaa, awọn iwọn iṣẹ jẹ:

- **Iṣẹ A**: $ P_A = 0.38 $
- **Iṣẹ B**: $ P_B = 0.29 $
- **Iṣẹ C**: $ P_C = 0.33 $

#### Awọn Ilana Ipele ati Iye Pinpin Tokeni ti o pọju

Da lori iwọn iṣẹ $ P_n $, iṣẹ kọọkan ni a ṣe akojọ si **Awọn ipele 1 nipasẹ 9**. Ipele yii nlo **cumulative top percentile** ti gbogbo awọn iwọn iṣẹ ti awọn iṣẹ.

##### Ọna Ipele Ipele

1. **Ṣiṣiro Awọn Iwọn Iṣẹ**

   Lo ọna ti a ṣalaye tẹlẹ lati ṣe iṣiro iwọn iṣẹ ti iṣẹ kọọkan $ P_n $.

2. **Ṣe Ipele Awọn Iṣẹ**

   Ṣeto gbogbo awọn iṣẹ ni **aṣẹ idinku** da lori awọn iwọn iṣẹ wọn.

3. **Ṣiṣiro Awọn Percentiles Top Cumulative**

   Fun iṣẹ kọọkan, ṣe iṣiro percentile oke ti o pọ si:

   $$
   \text{Cumulative Top Percentile (\%)} = \left(1 - \frac{\text{Rank} - 1}{N} \right) \times 100\%
   $$

   Nibo ni:

    - $ \text{Rank} $: Ipele iṣẹ (ti o bẹrẹ lati 1).
    - $ N $: Lapapọ nọmba awọn iṣẹ.

4. **Fi Awọn Ipele si**

   Fi awọn ipele si da lori awọn percentiles oke ti o pọ si:

    - **Ipele 1**: Cumulative top percentile > 96%
    - **Ipele 2**: 89% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 96%
    - **Ipele 3**: 77% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 89%
    - **Ipele 4**: 60% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 77%
    - **Ipele 5**: 40% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 60%
    - **Ipele 6**: 23% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 40%
    - **Ipele 7**: 11% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 23%
    - **Ipele 8**: 4% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 11%
    - **Ipele 9**: Cumulative top percentile ≤ 4%

**Apeere**:

Pẹlu apapọ ti **3** awọn iṣẹ:

1. **Ṣiṣiro Awọn Ipele ati Awọn Percentiles Top Cumulative**

    - **Iṣẹ A**:

        - Iwọn Iṣẹ: $ P_A = 0.38 $
        - Ipele: 1
        - Cumulative Top Percentile:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{1 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 100\%
          $$

    - **Iṣẹ C**:

        - Iwọn Iṣẹ: $ P_C = 0.33 $
        - Ipele: 2
        - Cumulative Top Percentile:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{2 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 66.67\%
          $$

    - **Iṣẹ B**:

        - Iwọn Iṣẹ: $ P_B = 0.29 $
        - Ipele: 3
        - Cumulative Top Percentile:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{3 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 33.33\%
          $$

2. **Fi Awọn Ipele si**

    - **Iṣẹ A**:

        - Cumulative Top Percentile: 100%
        - **Ipele**: **Ipele 1** (Cumulative Top Percentile > 96%)
        - **Maximum Token Distribution**: 30,000 Jet

    - **Iṣẹ C**:

        - Cumulative Top Percentile: 66.67%
        - **Ipele**: **Ipele 4** (60% < Cumulative Top Percentile ≤ 77%)
        - **Maximum Token Distribution**: 15,000 Jet

    - **Iṣẹ B**:

        - Cumulative Top Percentile: 33.33%
        - **Ipele**: **Ipele 6** (23% < Cumulative Top Percentile ≤ 40%)
        - **Maximum Token Distribution**: 7,000 Jet

**Awọn akọsilẹ**:

- **Awọn Iwọn Ti o Dọgba**: Awọn iṣẹ pẹlu awọn iwọn iṣẹ kanna gba ipo kanna. Ninu iṣiro awọn percentiles oke ti o pọ si, a lo ipo apapọ.
- **Maximum Token Distribution** tọka si iye tokeni ti o pọju ti iṣẹ le gba da lori ipele rẹ.

## Awọn Ere Olumulo ati Lilo Tokeni

### **Ẹrọ Ere Olumulo**

- **Awọn Ere ti o da lori Iṣẹ-ṣiṣe**: Awọn olumulo gba awọn tokeni JetPack nigbati wọn ṣe **awọn iṣẹ ti a gba tẹlẹ** (e.g., ẹda akoonu, ere) laarin iṣẹ kan.
- **Awọn Ilana Ere Tokeni**: Iye awọn tokeni ti a fun ni ere fun iṣẹ kọọkan jẹ **ipinnu nipasẹ ijumọsọrọ** laarin iṣẹ ati oniṣẹ eto JetPack.

### **Awọn ọna Lilo Tokeni**

1. **Ninu Iṣẹ naa**:
    - **Wọle si Awọn ẹya Ere**: Lo awọn tokeni lati wọle si awọn ẹya ti o sanwo tabi akoonu laarin iṣẹ naa.
    - **Ra Awọn Ọjà**: Ra awọn ọja oni-nọmba tabi awọn ohun inu iṣẹ nipa lilo awọn tokeni.

2. **Lilo Iṣowo Iṣẹ Apo JetPack**:
    - **Tita Tokeni**: Awọn olumulo le paarọ awọn tokeni wọn fun **ADA** (owo ti **Cardano**).
    - **Iṣakoso Apo**: Apo JetPack ngbanilaaye iṣakoso to ni aabo ti awọn tokeni ati awọn gbigbe si awọn iṣẹ miiran.

### **Awọn Anfaani ti a reti lati Iye Tokeni ti o niyelori**

- **Iṣan Tokeni**: Iṣowo awọn tokeni lori awọn paṣipaarọ mu ki wọn **niyelori**.
- **Awọn Ere Iṣuna**: Awọn olumulo le ṣaṣeyọri **awọn anfani eto-ọrọ afikun** nipa tita awọn tokeni ti iye wọn ba niyelori.

## Rira Tokeni ati Pinpin

### **Awọn Rira Tokeni afikun nipasẹ Awọn Iṣẹ**

- **Nigbati Awọn Tokeni ti a Pinpin ti pari**: Ti iṣẹ kan ba lo gbogbo awọn tokeni ti a pin si, o le ra awọn tokeni afikun lati **Iṣowo Iṣẹ Apo JetPack**.
- **Owo Rira**: Awọn tokeni ni a ra nipa lilo **ADA**.
- **Lilo ti Awọn Tokeni ti a ra**: Awọn tokeni ti a ra le ṣee lo lati **fun awọn olumulo ni ere** tabi **ninu iṣẹ naa**.

### **Awọn Tokeni ti a le ta pada ati Awọn Tokeni ti a ko le ta pada**

- **Awọn Tokeni ti a Pinpin** (**Ti a ko le ta pada**): Awọn tokeni ti a pin si awọn iṣẹ **ko le ta pada** ati pe o jẹ fun **awọn ere olumulo** nikan.
- **Awọn Tokeni ti a ra ati Awọn Tokeni ti a gba lati ọdọ Awọn olumulo** (**Ti a le ta pada**): Awọn tokeni ti a ra pẹlu ADA ati awọn tokeni ti a gba lati ọdọ awọn olumulo **le ta pada**, iranlọwọ ni **iṣowo niyelori**.

### **Pinpin Tokeni ati Igbesoke Eto**

1. **Eto Pinpin Tokeni**:
    - Awọn iṣẹ fun awọn olumulo ni awọn tokeni; awọn olumulo lo wọn laarin awọn iṣẹ tabi ta wọn lori awọn paṣipaarọ.
    - Awọn iṣẹ gba awọn tokeni lati ọdọ awọn olumulo tabi ra wọn lati ṣetọju **eto ere ti o ni atilẹyin**.

2. **Awọn ipa Igbesoke Eto**:
    - Pinpin tokeni ṣe igbega **ikopa olumulo** ati **igbesoke iṣẹ**.
    - Eyi ṣe alabapin si **iduroṣinṣin iye tokeni** ati **idagbasoke alagbero** ti eto naa.
