# Ilana Iṣẹ JetPack

Iranlọwọ ti Iṣẹ JetPack ni lati mọ “**Iduro Pẹlu Awọn Olumulo ati Awọn Iṣẹ**.”

**Ipele 1** ṣe alaye awọn ẹya pataki ati awọn akoko ipari ohun elo kọja ipele kọọkan, lati Ipele-1 si Ipele-5. Ni ipele yii, gbogbo awọn iṣẹ ṣiṣe pataki fun isopọmọ alailowaya laarin eto JetPack ati awọn iṣẹ rẹ yoo ni idagbasoke, pẹlu idojukọ lori ifilọlẹ awọn iṣẹ AI pataki ni ilọsiwaju. Ipele kọọkan ni itọsọna nipasẹ iṣẹ apinfunni ti **“AI-First Service Initiative”**.

**Ipele 2** yoo bẹrẹ ni kete ti Iṣẹ JetPack ti fihan iye to peye. Ipele yii yoo ṣafihan eto ipolowo ti o da lori ami JetPack ati awọn isopọmọ pẹlu awọn iru ẹrọ ẹnikẹta bii metaverse ati e-commerce. Eto alaye yoo kede nigbamii.


## Ipele 1

### **Ipele-1: Ipele 'Gagarin'**
**Ọjọ Ibẹrẹ**: Oṣu Kini 1, 2025

- Idagbasoke eto iṣakoso iṣẹ
  - Iṣẹ iforukọsilẹ ati yiyọ kuro
  - Imuse eto pinpin ami
- Idagbasoke oju opo wẹẹbu ati API REST ipilẹ

**Ipele Ti A Darukọ Lẹhin**: Yuri Gagarin, eniyan akọkọ ni aaye

---

### **Ipele-2: Ipele 'Shepard'**
**Akoko Ti A Nreti**: K3 2025

- Ifilọlẹ ohun elo iwe afọwọkọ **haru**
- Iṣẹ isalẹ ami **OG Degen Bunny**
- Ẹya gbigbe ami JetPack laarin awọn olumulo
- Isopọmọ atilẹyin apamọwọ ita

**Ipele Ti A Darukọ Lẹhin**: Alan Shepard, ara ilu Amẹrika akọkọ ni aaye

---

### **Ipele-3: Ipele 'Tereshkova'**
**Akoko Ti A Nreti**: K1 2026

- Ifilọlẹ **Iṣẹ Prompt**
- Idagbasoke **JetPack Token Exchange (CEX)**
- Idagbasoke **Iṣẹ NFT Drop**
- Gbooro SDK pupọ-ọrọ

**Ipele Ti A Darukọ Lẹhin**: Valentina Tereshkova, obinrin akọkọ ni aaye

---

### **Ipele-4: Ipele 'Armstrong'**
**Akoko Ti A Nreti**: K3 2026

- Ifilọlẹ awọn iṣẹ ti o ni ibatan si NFT
- Idagbasoke eto idibo awọn ofin ti o da lori **DAO**
  - Imuse awọn eto ipinnu aladani

**Ipele Ti A Darukọ Lẹhin**: Neil Armstrong, eniyan akọkọ lati tẹ ẹsẹ lori oṣupa

---

### **Ipele-5: Ipele 'Leonov'**
**Akoko Ti A Nreti**: K1 2027

- Ifilọlẹ awọn iṣẹ ti o ni ibatan si **DeFi**
- Mimu awọn olupese iṣẹ wa nipasẹ awọn apejọ ita
  - Awon ti a fojusi: awon akeko ile-iwe giga, awon olupilẹṣẹ nikan, ati be be lo.
- Imudara eto abojuto ati wiwa jegudujera (FDS)
  - Mimu iduroṣinṣin ati aabo eto lagbara

**Ipele Ti A Darukọ Lẹhin**: Alexei Leonov, eniyan akọkọ lati ṣe irin-ajo aaye
