# Sɛnea JetPack Tokens Yɛ Adwuma

JetPack token nhyehyɛ no fa **nsɛm a wɔyɛ no so na wɔde token ma ne sɛnea wɔbɔ no ho ban**, **asomdwoeɛ ahorow a wɔde yɛ adwuma**, **wɔde mafoɔ akatua ne sɛnea wɔde token yɛ adwuma**, ne **kwan a wɔfa so tɔ ne sɛnea wɔde token kyɛ**. Saa nhyehyɛ yi ma **akatua a ɛyɛ nokware ne a ɛyɛ adwuma** ma wɔn a wɔde yɛ adwuma ne wɔn a wɔyɛ adwuma no.

## Token Kyɛ (Drop) Kwan

### Token a Wɔde Bɛma ne Bere a Wɔde Bɛkyɛ

- **Token a Wɔde Bɛma**: JetPack bɛma **1 billion Jet** tokens nyinaa.
- **Bere a Wɔde Bɛkyɛ**: Wɔbɛkyɛ tokens no wɔ mfe **38** mu, a wɔbɛyɛ **2,776 kyɛ** wɔ bere a ɛyɛ pɔtee sɛ **Cardano Epoch** (1 epoch = nna 5).

### Token Kyɛ Dodow a Wɔde Bɛma Wɔ Epoch Biara Mu

#### Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Kyɛ Dodow No Firi Logarithmic Function So

Token dodow a wɔde bɛkyɛ wɔ epoch biara mu yɛ sɛ ɛbɛyɛ kakra kakra wɔ bere a ɛrekɔ so. **Logarithmic function** na wɔde bɔ kyɛ dodow no wɔ epoch biara mu. **1 Jet yɛ 1,000,000 Pack**, enti kyɛ dodow no wɔbɔ no kɔ **decimal a ɛto so nsia** na wɔde ma.

1. **Logarithmic Function $ f(t) $ Nkyerɛmu**:

   $$
   f(t) = \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

   Ɛhefa:

    - $ t $ yɛ epoch nɔma, $ t = 1, 2, 3, \dotsc, 2776 $.
    - $ \ln $ yɛ natural logarithm function.

2. **Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Log Function $ f(t) $ Sum Wɔ Epoch Biara Mu**:

   Sum a ɛyɛ nyinaa wɔ bere a wɔde bɛkyɛ no mu $ S $ yɛ:

   $$
   S = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} f(t) = \sum_{t=1}^{2776} \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

3. **Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Scaling Constant $ A $**:

   Token a wɔde bɛma a ɛyɛ **1 billion Jet** no wɔkyɛ sum $ S $ mu de yɛ epoch biara mu kyɛ dodow no:

   $$
   A = \dfrac{1,000,000,000}{S} = 2,412,005.7263929145
   $$

4. **Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Token Kyɛ Dodow Wɔ Epoch Biara Mu**:

   Kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɛma wɔ epoch biara mu yɛ:

   $$
   \text{Distribution Amount}_t = A \times f(t) = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(t + 1)}
   $$

5. **Kyɛ Dodow a Wɔde Bɛma No Nkyerɛmu**:

   Sɛnea wɔbɔ kyɛ dodow no wɔ epoch a edi kan no mu:

    - **Epoch 1**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_1 = 3,479,788.70 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(1 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 2**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_2 = 2,195,502.227 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(2 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3)}
      $$

    - **Epoch 3**:

      $$
      \text{Distribution Amount}_3 = 1,739,894.35 = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(3 + 1)} = A \times \dfrac{1}{\ln(4)}
      $$

   Kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɛma wɔ epoch biara mu yɛ log function a ɛfa epoch nɔma no ho.

**Kyɛ Nkyerɛmu**

- Esiane sɛ $ \ln(t + 1) $ kɔ soro wɔ bere a $ t $ kɔ soro nti, denominator no bɛyɛ kɛse, na ɛma kyɛ dodow no bɛyɛ kakra kakra.
- Kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɛma wɔ epoch biara mu bɛyɛ kakra kakra wɔ bere a ɛrekɔ so, na ɛbɛyɛ sɛ ɛrenyɛ hwee.
- Saa nhyehyɛ yi ma wɔn a wɔde yɛ adwuma a wɔyɛ kan no nya akatua na ɛma token no yɛ kakra.

### Token Kyɛ Ahyɛde Wɔ Asomdwoeɛ Biara Mu

#### Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Ahotoɔ Wɔ Asomdwoeɛ Mu

Ahotoɔ a wɔde bɛma wɔ asomdwoeɛ biara mu wɔbɔ no wɔ sɛnea ɛyɛ adwuma a ɛyɛ $ P_n $ fa ho sɛnea ɛfa adwuma a ɛyɛ nyinaa ho $ P $:

$$
\text{Ratio for Service } n = \frac{P_n}{P}
$$

Ɛhefa:

- $ P_n $: Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ $ n $.
- $ P $: Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ nyinaa sum.

#### Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Kyɛ Dodow Wɔ Asomdwoeɛ Biara Mu

Token dodow a asomdwoeɛ $ n $ bɛnya wɔ epoch biara mu $ S_n $ yɛ:

$$
S_n = \min\left(f_{\text{total}}(t) \times \left( \frac{P_n}{P} \right), \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n\right)
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t) $: Kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɔ wɔ epoch no mu.
- $ \text{Maximum Token Distribution for Service } n $: Token dodow a wɔde bɛma a ɛyɛ kɛse wɔ asomdwoeɛ no so (hwɛ level-based maximum token distribution a ɛda so wɔ nkyɛn).

#### Sɛnea Wɔyɛ Tokens a Wɔanfa Nkyɛ No Ho

##### Sɛnea Wɔde Tokens a Wɔanfa Nkyɛ No Kɔ So

- Tokens a wɔanfa nkyɛ wɔ epoch biara mu wɔde **ka** kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɛma wɔ epoch a ɛdi hɔ no ho.
- Eyi ma token kyɛ no yɛ adwuma sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ a asomdwoeɛ a wɔyɛ kan no bɛyɛ kakra.

##### Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Kyɛ Dodow a Wɔde Bɛma Wɔ Epoch a ɛdi Hɔ No Mu

Kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɛma wɔ epoch a ɛdi hɔ no mu yɛ:

$$
f_{\text{total}}(t+1) = f(t+1) + \text{Undistributed Tokens from Previous Epoch}
$$

- $ f_{\text{total}}(t+1) $: Kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɔ wɔ epoch $ t+1 $ mu.
- $ f(t+1) $: Kyɛ dodow a wɔde bɔ wɔ distribution function a ɛyɛ pɔtee so.
- **Tokens a Wɔanfa Nkyɛ No a Wɔde Kɔ So**: Tokens dodow a wɔanfa nkyɛ wɔ epoch $ t $ mu.

#### Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Adwuma a ɛyɛ ($ P_n $) Wɔ Asomdwoeɛ Biara Mu

Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ biara mu wɔbɔ no wɔ nsɛm a ɛda so yi so. Wɔtumi sesa nsɛm a ɛyɛ biara wɔ governance so.

1. **JetPack Token Consumption ($ M_{1n} $)**
    - **Weight**: $ w_1 = 50\% $
    - **Nkyerɛmu**: JetPack tokens dodow a wɔde yɛ adwuma wɔ asomdwoeɛ no mu.

2. **Degen Bunny Vote Count ($ M_{2n} $)**
    - **Weight**: $ w_2 = 30\% $
    - **Nkyerɛmu**: Nnɔma a wɔanya fi OG Degen Bunnies.

3. **Active User Count ($ M_{3n} $)**
    - **Weight**: $ w_3 = 20\% $
    - **Nkyerɛmu**: Nnɔma a ɛyɛ unique a wɔde yɛ adwuma wɔ asomdwoeɛ no mu wɔ epoch no mu.

Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ biara mu $ P_n $ wɔbɔ no sɛ:

$$
P_n = w_1 \times \left( \frac{M_{1n}}{M_{1\text{total}}} \right) + w_2 \times \left( \frac{M_{2n}}{M_{2\text{total}}} \right) + w_3 \times \left( \frac{M_{3n}}{M_{3\text{total}}} \right)
$$

Ɛhefa:

- $ M_{1n} $: JetPack token consumption a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ $ n $.
- $ M_{1\text{total}} $: JetPack token consumption a ɛyɛ nyinaa wɔ asomdwoeɛ nyinaa mu.
- $ M_{2n} $: Degen Bunny vote count a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ $ n $.
- $ M_{2\text{total}} $: Degen Bunny votes a ɛyɛ nyinaa wɔ asomdwoeɛ nyinaa mu.
- $ M_{3n} $: Active user count a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ $ n $.
- $ M_{3\text{total}} $: Active users a ɛyɛ nyinaa wɔ asomdwoeɛ nyinaa mu.

**Nkyerɛmu**:

- **Asomdwoeɛ A**:
    - $ M_{1A} = 500 $
    - $ M_{2A} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3A} = 200 $
- **Asomdwoeɛ B**:
    - $ M_{1B} = 300 $
    - $ M_{2B} = 200 $
    - $ M_{3B} = 100 $
- **Asomdwoeɛ C**:
    - $ M_{1C} = 200 $
    - $ M_{2C} = 150 $
    - $ M_{3C} = 700 $

**Totals**:

- $ M_{1\text{total}} = 500 + 300 + 200 = 1000 $
- $ M_{2\text{total}} = 150 + 200 + 150 = 500 $
- $ M_{3\text{total}} = 200 + 100 + 700 = 1000 $

**Weights**:

- $ w_1 = 0.5 $
- $ w_2 = 0.3 $
- $ w_3 = 0.2 $

**Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ Asomdwoeɛ A**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_A &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{500}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.5 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.2 \\
&= 0.25 + 0.09 + 0.04 \\
&= 0.38
\end{align*}
$$

**Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ Asomdwoeɛ B**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_B &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{300}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{200}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{100}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.3 + 0.3 \times 0.4 + 0.2 \times 0.1 \\
&= 0.15 + 0.12 + 0.02 \\
&= 0.29
\end{align*}
$$

**Sɛnea Wɔbɔ Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ Asomdwoeɛ C**:

$$
\begin{align*}
P_C &= 0.5 \times \left( \frac{200}{1000} \right) + 0.3 \times \left( \frac{150}{500} \right) + 0.2 \times \left( \frac{700}{1000} \right) \\
&= 0.5 \times 0.2 + 0.3 \times 0.3 + 0.2 \times 0.7 \\
&= 0.10 + 0.09 + 0.14 \\
&= 0.33
\end{align*}
$$

Enti, adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ no yɛ:

- **Asomdwoeɛ A**: $ P_A = 0.38 $
- **Asomdwoeɛ B**: $ P_B = 0.29 $
- **Asomdwoeɛ C**: $ P_C = 0.33 $

#### Level Determination Criteria ne Maximum Token Distribution Amount

Wɔde adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ $ P_n $ no, asomdwoeɛ biara wɔde no yɛ **Levels 1 kɔ 9**. Wɔde **cumulative top percentile** a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ nyinaa adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ no yɛ.

##### Cumulative Top Percentiles ne Maximum Token Distribution Amounts wɔ Level Biara Mu

| **Level** | **Cumulative Top Percentile** | **Maximum Token Distribution (Jet)** |
|-----------|-------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Level 1   | Top 4%                        | 30,000                               |
| Level 2   | Top 11%                       | 25,000                               |
| Level 3   | Top 23%                       | 20,000                               |
| Level 4   | Top 40%                       | 15,000                               |
| Level 5   | Top 60%                       | 10,000                               |
| Level 6   | Top 77%                       | 7,000                                |
| Level 7   | Top 89%                       | 5,000                                |
| Level 8   | Top 96%                       | 3,000                                |
| Level 9   | Top 100%                      | 1,000                                |

##### Level Determination Method

1. **Bɔ Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ Scores**

   Fa ɔkwan a wɔde bɔ adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ asomdwoeɛ biara mu $ P_n $ no yɛ.

2. **Determine Service Rankings**

   Fa asomdwoeɛ nyinaa yɛ **descending order** wɔ adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ scores so.

3. **Bɔ Cumulative Top Percentiles**

   Wɔ asomdwoeɛ biara mu, bɔ cumulative top percentile:

   $$
   \text{Cumulative Top Percentile (\%)} = \left(1 - \frac{\text{Rank} - 1}{N} \right) \times 100\%
   $$

   Ɛhefa:

    - $ \text{Rank} $: Asomdwoeɛ no rank (fi 1).
    - $ N $: Asomdwoeɛ a ɛyɛ nyinaa dodow.

4. **Assign Levels**

   Fa levels yɛ wɔ cumulative top percentiles so:

    - **Level 1**: Cumulative top percentile > 96%
    - **Level 2**: 89% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 96%
    - **Level 3**: 77% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 89%
    - **Level 4**: 60% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 77%
    - **Level 5**: 40% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 60%
    - **Level 6**: 23% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 40%
    - **Level 7**: 11% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 23%
    - **Level 8**: 4% < Cumulative top percentile ≤ 11%
    - **Level 9**: Cumulative top percentile ≤ 4%

**Nkyerɛmu**:

Wɔ asomdwoeɛ a ɛyɛ **3**:

1. **Bɔ Rankings ne Cumulative Top Percentiles**

    - **Asomdwoeɛ A**:

        - Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ Score: $ P_A = 0.38 $
        - Rank: 1
        - Cumulative Top Percentile:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{1 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 100\%
          $$

    - **Asomdwoeɛ C**:

        - Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ Score: $ P_C = 0.33 $
        - Rank: 2
        - Cumulative Top Percentile:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{2 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 66.67\%
          $$

    - **Asomdwoeɛ B**:

        - Adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ Score: $ P_B = 0.29 $
        - Rank: 3
        - Cumulative Top Percentile:

          $$
          \text{Cumulative Top Percentile} = \left(1 - \frac{3 - 1}{3} \right) \times 100\% = 33.33\%
          $$

2. **Assign Levels**

    - **Asomdwoeɛ A**:

        - Cumulative Top Percentile: 100%
        - **Level**: **Level 1** (Cumulative Top Percentile > 96%)
        - **Maximum Token Distribution**: 30,000 Jet

    - **Asomdwoeɛ C**:

        - Cumulative Top Percentile: 66.67%
        - **Level**: **Level 4** (60% < Cumulative Top Percentile ≤ 77%)
        - **Maximum Token Distribution**: 15,000 Jet

    - **Asomdwoeɛ B**:

        - Cumulative Top Percentile: 33.33%
        - **Level**: **Level 6** (23% < Cumulative Top Percentile ≤ 40%)
        - **Maximum Token Distribution**: 7,000 Jet

**Notes**:

- **Tied Scores**: Asomdwoeɛ a wɔanya adwuma a ɛyɛ a ɛyɛ scores a ɛyɛ pɔtee no nya rank a ɛyɛ pɔtee. Wɔ bɔ cumulative top percentiles no mu, wɔde average rank yɛ.
- **Maximum Token Distribution** yɛ token dodow a ɛyɛ kɛse a asomdwoeɛ biara tumi nya wɔ level a ɛyɛ no so.

## Wɔde Mafoɔ Akatua ne Token Yɛ Adwuma

### **Wɔde Mafoɔ Akatua Nhyehyɛ**

- **Akatua a Wɔde Ma Wɔ Nsɛm a Wɔyɛ So**: Wɔde JetPack tokens ma wɔ nsɛm a wɔyɛ **a wɔapene so dedaw** (e.g., content creation, gameplay) wɔ asomdwoeɛ mu.
- **Token Akatua Ahyɛde**: Token dodow a wɔde ma wɔ nsɛm biara mu wɔ **yɛ wɔ asɛm a wɔyɛ ne JetPack ecosystem operator ntam**.

### **Token Yɛ Adwuma Kwan**

1. **Wɔ Asomdwoeɛ Mu**:
    - **Kɔ Premium Features**: Fa tokens yɛ adwuma kɔ features a wɔyɛ sika anaa nsɛm a wɔyɛ wɔ asomdwoeɛ mu.
    - **Tɔ Nnwuma**: Tɔ digital goods anaa nsɛm a wɔyɛ wɔ asomdwoeɛ mu de tokens yɛ adwuma.

2. **Fa JetPack Wallet Service Exchange Yɛ Adwuma**:
    - **Token Tɔn**: Wɔtumi sesa wɔn tokens de yɛ **ADA** (sika a ɛyɛ **Cardano**).
    - **Wallet Management**: JetPack wallet ma wɔtumi yɛ tokens ho ban na wɔtumi fa kɔ asomdwoeɛ foforɔ mu.

### **Sika a Wɔnya fi Token Value a ɛyɛ Dɛ No Ho Mfasoɔ a Wɔhwɛ**

- **Token Liquidity**: Sɛ wɔtɔn tokens wɔ exchanges a ɛma wɔn **liquidity**.
- **Sika a Wɔnya**: Wɔtumi nya **sika foforɔ a ɛyɛ dɛ** sɛ wɔtɔn tokens no sɛ wɔn value yɛ dɛ.

## Token Tɔ ne Kyɛ

### **Asomdwoeɛ a Wɔtɔ Tokens Foforɔ**

- **Sɛ Tokens a Wɔde Bɛkyɛ No Asa**: Sɛ asomdwoeɛ bi de tokens a wɔde bɛkyɛ no nyinaa yɛ adwuma a, wɔtumi tɔ tokens foforɔ fi **JetPack Wallet Service Exchange**.
- **Tɔ Sika**: Wɔtɔ tokens de **ADA** yɛ adwuma.
- **Tokens a Wɔtɔ No Yɛ Adwuma**: Tokens a wɔtɔ no wɔtumi de yɛ **wɔde mafoɔ akatua** anaa **wɔ asomdwoeɛ mu**.

### **Tokens a Wɔtumi Tɔn ne Tokens a Wɔntumi Tɔn**

- **Tokens a Wɔde Bɛkyɛ** (**Wɔntumi Tɔn**): Tokens a wɔde ma asomdwoeɛ **wɔntumi tɔn** na wɔde yɛ **wɔde mafoɔ akatua**.
- **Tokens a Wɔtɔ ne Tokens a Wɔnya fi Wɔde Mafoɔ** (**Wɔtumi Tɔn**): Tokens a wɔtɔ de ADA ne tokens a wɔnya fi wɔde mafoɔ **wɔtumi tɔn**, a ɛboa wɔ **liquidity acquisition**.

### **Token Kyɛ ne Ecosystem Activation**

1. **Token Circulation Nhyehyɛ**:
    - Asomdwoeɛ de tokens ma wɔde mafoɔ; wɔde mafoɔ de yɛ wɔ asomdwoeɛ mu anaa wɔtɔn wɔ exchanges.
    - Asomdwoeɛ nya tokens fi wɔde mafoɔ anaa wɔtɔ de yɛ adwuma ma **akatua a ɛyɛ dɛ**.

2. **Ecosystem Activation Mfasoɔ**:
    - Token circulation ma **wɔde mafoɔ yɛ adwuma** ne **asomdwoeɛ activation**.
    - Eyi ma **token value yɛ dɛ** na ɛboa **ecosystem nkɔsoɔ a ɛyɛ dɛ**.
