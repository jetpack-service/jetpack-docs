# Chiyambi

M'masiku ano a digito, ogwiritsa ntchito amalumikizana ndi ntchito zambiri za pa intaneti ndi mapulogalamu tsiku ndi tsiku. Komabe, ngakhale kutenga nawo mbali kwawo, ogwiritsa ntchito nthawi zambiri samalandira malipiro okwanira kapena palibe chifukwa cha zopereka zawo ndi kulumikizana. Nthawi yomweyo, opereka ntchito, makamaka opanga odziyimira pawokha ndi oyambitsa, amakumana ndi zovuta zazikulu pakukhazikitsa ndi kusamalira machitidwe a mfundo zawo chifukwa cha mtengo waukulu wa chitukuko ndi zovuta. Machitidwe a mfundo zogawana alipo kale amabwera ndi zopinga zolowera zolimba, malipiro ovuta, ndi kutayika kwa ndalama pomwe mfundo zomwe zapezedwa kuchokera ku ntchito imodzi zimagwiritsidwa ntchito mu ina. Kuphatikiza apo, machitidwe achikhalidwe a tokeni amakhala osakondera, kugawa kuchuluka kwa ma tokeni kwa omwe adayika ndalama koyambirira ndi opanga, kusiya ogwiritsa ntchito wamba osakondera kuyambira pachiyambi.

**JetPack Token System** imawonekera ngati yankho la mavuto awa omwe amapezeka powonjezera chilengedwe chachilungamo, chowonekera, komanso chogwira ntchito bwino chomwe chimapindulitsa ogwiritsa ntchito ndi opereka ntchito.

## Mafotokozedwe a Vuto

- **Kusowa kwa Mphotho kwa Ogwiritsa Ntchito**: Ogwiritsa ntchito samalandira malipiro okwanira chifukwa cha kutenga nawo mbali ndi zochita zawo mkati mwa ntchito za pa intaneti ndi mapulogalamu, zomwe zimachepetsa chilimbikitso chawo kutenga nawo mbali mwachangu.
- **Zovuta za Opereka Ntchito**: Kupanga ndi kusamalira machitidwe a mfundo zawo kumafuna zinthu zambiri, kumafuna nthawi yochuluka ndi ndalama, ndipo kungakhale ngongole yazachuma.
- **Zolepheretsa za Machitidwe a Mfundo Alipo**: Machitidwe a mfundo zogawana nthawi zambiri amakhala ndi zofunikira zolowera, ndipo pamene ogwiritsa ntchito amagwiritsa ntchito mfundo zomwe apeza kuchokera ku ntchito imodzi ku ina, zimatha kubweretsa kuchepa kwa phindu kwa ntchito yoyambirira ndi njira zovuta zolandila.
- **Machitidwe a Tokeni Achikhalidwe Osakondera**: Machitidwe ambiri a tokeni amakondera kwambiri omwe adayika ndalama koyambirira ndi opanga powapatsa kuchuluka kwa ma tokeni, kuletsa mpikisano wolungama ndi mwayi wofanana kwa ogwiritsa ntchito wamba.

## Chidule cha Yankho

**JetPack Token System** imapereka njira yatsopano yothandizira mphotho za digito ndi machitidwe a mfundo mwa:

- **Kukhazikitsa Chitsanzo Chogawa Tokeni Chachilungamo ndi Chowonekera**: Onse omwe akutenga nawo mbali amayamba pamalo ofanana, chifukwa palibe kugawa ma tokeni kwa aliyense, kuphatikizapo opanga ndi omwe adayika ndalama koyambirira.
- **Kupereka Tokeni Yosinthika Yogwiritsidwa Ntchito M'mautumiki Ambiri**: Ogwiritsa ntchito amatha kupeza ndi kugwiritsa ntchito JetPack Tokens m'mautumiki osiyanasiyana, kukulitsa ntchito ndi mtengo wa ma tokeni.
- **Kutsimikizira Kuthamanga Kwambiri kwa Zochitika ndi Chitetezo**: Pophatikiza ma database ogawidwa ndiukadaulo wa blockchain, JetPack imapeza kuthamanga kwakukulu kwa zochitika (TPS) pomwe ikusunga umphumphu wa deta ndi chitetezo.
- **Kuyambitsa Njira ya Mulingo wa Ntchito Kuti Alimbikitse Kukula**: Ntchito zimapeza ma tokeni masiku asanu aliwonse (epoch), ndi mulingo wawo ukuwonjezeka kutengera kugwiritsa ntchito ma tokeni ndi ogwiritsa ntchito, zomwe zimawalola kupeza ma tokeni ambiri ndikulimbikitsa kutenga nawo mbali kwa ogwiritsa ntchito.

## Phindu Lapadera

- **Mwayi Wolungama ndi Wofanana kwa Onse Ogwiritsa Ntchito**: JetPack imachotsa mwayi osakondera omwe amawoneka m'machitidwe achikhalidwe a tokeni popanda kugawa ma tokeni kwa aliyense pasadakhale.
- **Kuphweka kwa Kulumikiza kwa Opereka Ntchito**: Opanga amatha kulumikiza mosavuta JetPack Token System mu ntchito zawo pogwiritsa ntchito ma SDK ndi ma API omwe aperekedwa, kupulumutsa nthawi ndi zinthu.
- **Kulimbikitsa Kutenga Nawo Mbali kwa Ogwiritsa Ntchito ndi Mphotho**: Ogwiritsa ntchito amalipidwa mwachindunji chifukwa cha zochita zawo, zomwe zimawonjezera chilimbikitso chawo kutenga nawo mbali ndi ntchito.
- **Zochitika Zogwira Ntchito ndi Zotetezeka**: Kuphatikiza kwa ma database ogawidwa ndi blockchain kumatsimikizira kuti zochitika zikuchitika mwachangu komanso motetezeka popanda kuwononga umphumphu wa deta.
- **Zolimbikitsa Kukula kwa Ntchito**: Njira ya mulingo imapereka mphotho kwa ntchito zomwe zimalimbikitsa kutenga nawo mbali kwa ogwiritsa ntchito ndi kugwiritsa ntchito ma tokeni, kulimbikitsa chilengedwe chathanzi komanso chamipikisano.

## Momwe Zimagwirira Ntchito

- **Kugawa Tokeni ndi Njira ya Mulingo**: Masiku asanu aliwonse, ntchito zimapeza kugawa kwa JetPack Tokens. Ntchito zimatha kukweza mulingo wawo kuchokera ku 0 mpaka 99 kutengera kuchuluka kwa ma tokeni omwe agwiritsidwa ntchito ndi ogwiritsa ntchito awo, zomwe zimawalola kupeza ma tokeni owonjezera m'magawo otsatirawa.
- **Njira ya Mphotho ya Ogwiritsa Ntchito**: Ogwiritsa ntchito amapeza JetPack Tokens kudzera mu zochita zawo mkati mwa ntchito. Atha kugwiritsa ntchito ma tokeni awa mkati mwa chilengedwe cha ntchito kapena kuwagulitsa m'misika kuti apeze ADA, kupereka phindu lenileni la kulumikizana kwawo kwa digito.
- **Kugula ndi Kugawa Ma Tokeni**: Ntchito ikatha kugwiritsa ntchito ma tokeni omwe agawidwa, imatha kugula ma tokeni owonjezera pogwiritsa ntchito ADA kuti ipitilize kupereka mphotho kwa ogwiritsa ntchito ake, kuonetsetsa kuti pali kuzungulira kosalekeza kwa mphotho.
- **Kuchita Zochitika Mwachangu ndi Njira Zotetezera**: Deta ya zochitika imasungidwa mu database yogawidwa ndipo imatetezedwa pogwiritsa ntchito kapangidwe ka unyolo wa hash kuti tipewe kusokoneza. Kuphatikiza apo, mphindi zisanu zilizonse, mtengo wa hash umasungidwa pa blockchain ya Cardano kuti atsimikizire umphumphu wa deta.
