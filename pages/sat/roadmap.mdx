# JetPack Service Roadmap

JetPack Service re vision achhi “**Users ar Services majh Coexistence**.”

**Phase 1** re sab se jaruri features ar app completion timelines ke outline kora achhi, Stage-1 se Stage-5 tak. Ehi phase re, JetPack system ar services majh seamless integration re sab jaruri functionalities ke develop kora jabe, core AI-driven services ke progressively launch kora focus re. Pratyek stage ke guide kora jabe **“AI-First Service Initiative”** mission se.

**Phase 2** JetPack Service ke sufficient value demonstrate kora pare commence kora jabe. Ehi phase re JetPack ke token-based advertising system ar metaverse ar e-commerce jaise third-party platforms se integrations introduce kora jabe. Ek detailed schedule pare announce kora jabe.
