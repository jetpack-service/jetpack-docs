# JetPack Service Cookie Policy

JetPack Service utilizes **cookies** and similar technologies to ensure smooth operation and enhancement of its website and services. These technologies improve user experience, analyze site performance, and support authentication, personalization, and more.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored by a website on your browser to remember user preferences and other information. They enable the website to provide a better experience when users revisit.

## Types of Cookies Used by JetPack Service

JetPack Service uses the following types of cookies:

### 1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary to provide basic functionality and maintain the security of the website. Without them, the service may not function properly.

- **Authentication Cookies**: Maintain user login status and provide secure access to accounts.
  - **Google OAuth Cookies**: Used for authentication and session maintenance through Google accounts.
  - **Supabase Authentication Cookies**: Manage user authentication and sessions.

### 2. Performance Cookies

These cookies help improve the website's performance by collecting anonymous information about visitor numbers, page views, and usage patterns, enabling site optimization.

- **Analytics Cookies**: Use internal or third-party tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience.

### 3. Functional Cookies

These cookies remember user preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience.

- **Language Preference Cookies**: Store selected language settings for consistent service on subsequent visits.
- **Dark Mode Setting Cookies**: Save user interface preferences, such as dark or light mode.

### 4. Targeting Cookies

These cookies deliver more relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns. JetPack Service does not currently use targeting cookies but will update this policy if they are implemented in the future.

## Third-Party Cookies

JetPack Service may use cookies provided by trusted third parties to enhance its services. These cookies are set by third-party providers (e.g., Google, Supabase) to support authentication, analytics, and other functionalities.
